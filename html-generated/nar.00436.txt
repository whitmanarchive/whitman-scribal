            February 13, 1868.
            Hon. O. H. Browning,
            Secretary of the Interior.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to Three hundred seventy five dollars, of Wm. C. Bunts, for legal services as assistant to F. J. Dickman, Esq. United States Attorney for the Northern District of Ohio, from November 9th, 1867 to February 9th, 1868; and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund.
            Very Respectfully,
            your obedient servant,
            Henry Stanbery
            Attorney General.
