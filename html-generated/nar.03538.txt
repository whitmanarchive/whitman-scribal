            Dec. 15, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the President to request you to issue a commission (agreeably to the enclosed resolution of the Senate,) appointing George Andrews to the office of Attorney of the United States for the Eastern District of Tennessee; George C. Bates to the office of Attorney of the U.S. for the Territory of Utah; and James Neville to the office of Attorney of the U.S. for the District of Nebraska.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
