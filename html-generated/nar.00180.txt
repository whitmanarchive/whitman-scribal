            January 12, 1867.
            Geo. G. Munger Esq.
            U. S. Attorney, Rochester, N. Y.
            Sir:
            You are hereby directed to stay all proceedings in the prosecution for smuggling, now pending in your District, against George A. Parker. This instruction is given at the request of the Secretary of the Treasury.
            Very Respectfully,
            Your obd't serv't,
            J. Hubley Ashton
            Ass't Att'y Gen'l.
