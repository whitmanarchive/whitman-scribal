            April 17, 1869.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a Report made by the U. S. Marshal for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, to the U. S. Attorney at Philadelphia in relation to the steamer Florida, referred to in your letter to me of the 3d instant.
            With great respect, y'r ob't servt
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
