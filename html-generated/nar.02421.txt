            Aug. 23, 1871.
            David Broveis, Esq.
            Luda P. O. Ill.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney Gen'l to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 20th inst. asking for the Acts of the 2d Session of the 41st Congress, and to say to you that this Department has no such documents for public distribution.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk
            no such books to send.
