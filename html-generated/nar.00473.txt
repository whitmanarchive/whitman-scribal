           March 17, 1868.
            The President,
            Sir:
            In compliance with your order of the 17th instant, I have the honor to transmit herewith the Resolution of the Senate of the 12th instant, referred to therein.
            With great respect, your obedient serv't,
            John M. Binckley
            Assistant Attorney General.
