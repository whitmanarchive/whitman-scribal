            Nov. 23, 1871.
            C. W. Rowell, Esq.
            U. S. Attorney, Arizona City, Arizona Terrr.Terr.
            Sir:
            I have received from you five letters, dated November 1st, 1871; one in relation to the Camp Grant masacre ; one in relation to the case of Governor Safford; one in relation to Bashford, the Secretary; one in relation to the Surveyor General; and one in relation to the late Marshal Phelps; all of which will receive proper consideration—and when they affect persons connected with other Departments the information which you have conveyed will be transmitted to the Heads of those Departments.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            P. S. The original letters enclosed in yours are herewith returned as you requested.
            five letters acknowledged
            Arizona
