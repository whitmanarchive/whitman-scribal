            Dec. 14, 1871.
            Hon. Lyman Trumbull,
            U.S. Senate.
            Sir:
            At the request of Senator Clayton, I send you all the papers on file in this Department relating to the removal of Isaac C. Mills, formerly U.S. Marshal for the Eastern District of Arkansas.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            P. S. Please return the papers when no longer needed by the Committee.
            
            papers sent
