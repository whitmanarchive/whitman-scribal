            May 22, 1871.
            Messrs. Pike &amp; Johnson,
            Attorneys at Law,
            Washington, D. C.
            Sirs:
            The enclosed copy of a letter from me to the District Attorney of the Western District of Arkansas, will disclose to you my action in reference to your application in reference for a dismissal of the criminal proceedings against Elias C. Boudinot.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            E. C. Boudinot's case. 
            see Ins. Bk. B. p 188.
