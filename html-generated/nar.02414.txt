            Aug. 19, 1871.
            Hon. J. G. Palen,
            Chief Justice of the Supreme Court,
            Elizabethtown, Colfax Co. 
            New Mexico.
            Sir:
            In answer to your letter of the 9th instant, I have the honor to say that I do not feel at liberty, under the Executive Order of March 31, 1871, a copy of which has been sent you, to accord the leave of absence which you desire.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            leave declined
            New Mex
