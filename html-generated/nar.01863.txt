           May 9, 1871.
           A. B. McConoughey, Esq.
           Nevada, Vernon Co. Missouri.
           Sir:
           Your letter of the 11th ultimo has been referred to the officer having charge of the detective system, and I hope it may be useful in aiding in the detection of crime.
          Very respectfully,
           A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            detective information
