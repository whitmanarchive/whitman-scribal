            Dec. 15, 1871.
            G. Willey Wells, Esq. U. S. Attorney
            Holly Springs, M'pi.
            Sir:
            In pursuance of the request
           
            B. L. Lee Special 
            
            
            
            in your letter of the 11th instant, in which General Ames urgently concurs, I herewith enclose a commission for Mr. Lee.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney Gen'l.
