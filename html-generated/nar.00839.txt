           March 15, 1869.
            Dunbar, Waters, &amp; Co,
            Publishers,Boston, Mass.
            Gentlemen:
            The Attorney General desires you to send to this office a copy of the Boston Daily Advertiser. As our bills are paid by the Treasury Dept. if you will send me duplicate receipted bills, I will return the money at once.
            Respectfully,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Clerk.
