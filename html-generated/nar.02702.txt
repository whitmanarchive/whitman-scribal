            Dec. 30, 1871.
            J. O. Green, Esq.
            Washington, D. C.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that your resignation as Chief Clerk of the Office of the Solicitor of the Treasury, has been received and accepted, to take effect on the 31st instant.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
            resignation accepted.
