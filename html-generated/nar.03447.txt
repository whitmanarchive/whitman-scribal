            Nov. 4, 1871.
            Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to acknowledge, with thanks, the receipt of your favor of the 3d instant, with checks, paid by the Assistant Treasurer of New York, enclosed.
            Very respectfully,
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l,
            &amp; Acting Attorney Gen'l.
