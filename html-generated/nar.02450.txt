           Sept. 6, 1871.
            Mr. W. H. Baker,
            Savannah, Geo.
            Sir:
            I have received your letter of the 29th ultimo, and in reply inform you that several weeks ago a direction was given from this office to dismiss the appeal in the case of the cotton claim of the estate of J. W. Morrell, which I presume
                appeal dismissed
                Geo. 
            
            is the case referred to in your letter.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
