            February 4, 1868,
            B. F. Penniman, Esq.
            Care of Hatch, Estes &amp; Co. p.o. Box 6155,
            New York City.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 3d, enclosing a copy of your letter of the same date to the Assistant District Attorney at Boston has been received by the Attorney General.
            I herewith enclose a copy of our Instructions of this date to the United States District Attorney at Boston.
            Respectfully, your obedient serv't,
            John M. Binckley,
            Assistant District Attorney.
            [copy enclosed.]
            
            [For letter to the President, February 4, with Report &amp; List of Justices of Peace, see p. 73.]
            see Ins. Book pp. 19, 20
