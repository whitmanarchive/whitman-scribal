            December 30, 1870.
            Hon. Nathan Sargent,
            Washington, D. C.
            Sir:
            By direction of the Solicitor General, and acting Attorney General, I transmit herewith a Commission, appointing you to the office of member of the Levy Court of the County of Washington, District of Columbia.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk
            Commission
