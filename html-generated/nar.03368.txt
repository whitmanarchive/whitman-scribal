            Oct. 5, 1871.
            Hon. J. C. B. Davis,
            Acting Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to acknowledge
            
            
            
            the receipt of your letter of the 4th inst. transmitting certain papers relative to the attack upon the house and person of the Spanish Consul at Key West, Florida, and to inform you that the U.S. Attorney for the Southern District of Florida has been directed to take such measures as may be necessary to bring the guilty parties to punishment.
            Very respectfully,
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l
            &amp; Acting Attorney Gen'l.
            
            attack on Spanish Consul
