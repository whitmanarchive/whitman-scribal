            January 17, 1870.
            Messrs. T. &amp; J. W. Johnson &amp; Co.
            Philadelphia, Pa.
            Gentlemen:
            In compliance with your request of the 8th inst, I herewith enclose the list of books some time since returned by you to this office, with prices indicated thereon. Accompanying it is also a list showing such of these books as are still needed, exclusive of what have been already ordered, but not delivered.
            Very resp. your obd't servt,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Cl'k.
            Library Books.
