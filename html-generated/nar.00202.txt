           January 29, 1867.
Hon. W. H. Seward,
Secretary of State.
Sir:
I am directed by the President to request you to issue a Commission appointing George S. Hillard to be Attorney of the United States, in accordance with the enclosed resolution of the Senate.
Respectfully, your obd't Serv't
Henry Stanbery
Attorney General.
