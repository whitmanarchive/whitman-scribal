            May 3, 1869.
            Hon. John A. Rawlins,
            Secretary of War.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a letter dated May 1st, from A. J. Herr, Esq. of Harrisburg, Pa, relative to the case of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania vs. Serg't. C. W. Frazer, charged with kidnapping &amp; conspiracy—in which Mr. Herr was employed in accordance with your request of the 20th ult.
            Very Resp. Sir, &amp;c. &amp;c.
            W. A. Field, Acting A. G.
