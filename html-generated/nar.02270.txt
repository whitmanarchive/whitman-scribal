            Dec. 20, 1871.
            E. L. Kearney, Esq.
            U. S. Marshal,
            Walla Walla, W. T.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 30th ult. containing your sworn statement in regard to the escape of John R. Smith, has been received.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            statement rec'd
