            Aug. 25, 1871.
            Hon. J. R. Lewis
            Washington, Iowa.
            Sir:
            In pursuance of the request in your letter of the 21st instant, the forged letter of resignation will be sent to the District Attorney of Idaho.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            forged letter
            Idaho
