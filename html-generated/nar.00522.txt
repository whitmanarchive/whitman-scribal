            April 30, 1868.
            W. W. Webb, Esq.
            President Central American Transit Co.
            56 Exchange Place New York
            Sir:
          In reply to your letter of 28th instant, you are informed that it must be several days before the case referred by the Secretary of State under date of the 25th instant, can be reached, in the order of business for action in this office.
            Respectfully, your obedient servant,
            John M. Binckley,
            Assistant Attorney General.
