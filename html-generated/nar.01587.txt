            December 30, 1870.
            G. H. Kingsbury, Esq.
            Boston, Mass.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Hon. Clement Hugh Hill, Assistant Attorney General, to inform you that the answer of the Attorney General to the Secretary of the Treasury, relative to the tea duties, has been sent in.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. J. Falls.
            Chief Clerk.
            official information
