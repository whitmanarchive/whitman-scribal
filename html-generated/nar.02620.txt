            Nov. 25, 1871.
            William Grant, Esq.
            New Orleans, La.
            Sir:
            You are hereby retained as special assistant to the U.S. Attorney for Louisiana, in the following cases in the Circuit Court: The U.S. vs. W. R. Whitaker, et al.; and The U.S. vs. Thomas P. May et al.
            Special U.S. Counsel La.
          La.
            
            
            For this service, a reasonable compensation will be paid you, to be determined by this Department.
            You will take and transmit to this office the oath required by law of District Attorneys.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
