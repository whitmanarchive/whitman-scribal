            Nov. 3, 1871.
            S. R. Harlow, Esq, U. S. Marshal.
            Brooklyn, New York.
            Sir: The subject of your communication of the 30th ult. in relation to the prosecution of one of your Deputies for acts alleged to have been committed in the line of his official duty, has been referred to the Dist. Attorney of your Dist. with whom I desire you should confer on the subject. Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General
                        
            Case of Dep. Marshal
            Ea. N. Y.
