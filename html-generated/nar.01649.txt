            January 20, 1871.
            Hon. P. S. Smith
            Third Auditor of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith my accounts as Disbursing Clerk for the Department of Justice for the quarter ending December 31, 1870.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. J. Falls,
            Disbursing Clerk.
            Acc'ts transmitted
