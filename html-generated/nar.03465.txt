            Nov. 11, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the President to request you to issue a commission appointing James N. Kerns, to the office of Marshal of the U.S. for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, in place of E. M. Gregory, deceased.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
