            Nov. 16, 1871.
            G. C. Wharton, Esq. U. S. Attorney,
            Louisville, Ky.
            Sir:
            I enclose certain additional papers transmitted from the War Department, in relation to the fraudulent transactions of B. F. Wayne, late Lieutenant and Regimental Quartermaster 3d Kentucky Volunteers.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            B. F. Wayne's case
