           March 17, 1869.
            The Honorable the Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            In reply to your letter of the 16th inst. relative to the matter of the pardon of Jacob and Moses Depuy, I have the honor to enclose herewith a copy of a letter of instructions this day addressed to the Attorney of the U. S. for the Southern Dist. of New York.
            Very resp. yours, &amp;c.
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
