           March 10, 1868.
            James W. Locke, Esq.
            Clerk U. S. District Court,
            Key West, Florida.
            Sir:
            Would you do us the favor to send to this office, at once a certificate of the judgment, and that an appeal was allowed to claimant in prize case of Schooner John Williams, giving full name of case, name of claimant and dates of payment and appeal?
            Respectfully, your obedient servant,
            John M. Binckley,
            Assistant Attorney Gen'l
