            January 13, 1870.
            To General Adelbert Ames,
            Jackson,
            Mississippi.
            I think the Senators should be chosen on the second Tuesday of the session, but do not feel confident that the Senate would regard this as imperative.
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General
            (ch'ge)
            Tel.
            State of Mississippi U. S. Senators
