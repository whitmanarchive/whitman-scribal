            Dec. 22, 1866.
            Geo. M. Pinney, Esq.
            U. S. Marshal, Montana T.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney Gen'l. to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence, agreeably to your request of the 21st inst. from Nov. 7th, 1866, until Feb. 15, 1867
            Respectfully, &amp;c
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Clerk
