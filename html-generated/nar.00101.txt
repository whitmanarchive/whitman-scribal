           Attorney General's Office,
            April 12, 1866.
            To the President.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith an abstract of the persons who recommended Duff Green for Marshal,—&amp; also the papers of recommendation.
            I am sir, very respectfully,
            Your obedient servant,
            James Speed
            Attorney General.
