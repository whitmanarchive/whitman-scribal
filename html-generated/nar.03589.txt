            Dec. 25, 1871.
            Hon. D. M. DuBose,
            House of Representatives.
            Sir: I have received your letter of the 21st instant, and in compliance with your request return herewith the papers relative to the claim of Col. John B. Weems which were transmitted to this Department by you on the 7th instant
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
            P. S. As the original letter of Mr. Morrill to me should remain on file, I send a copy.
            A. T. A.
            
            claim of J. B. Weems, (papers returned)
