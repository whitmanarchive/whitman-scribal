            September 21, 1869.
            Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            In reply to your letter of the 31st ultimo, I have the honor to transmit herewith the estimate of appropriations required for the service of this Department for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1871.
            Very respectfully,
            your obedient servant,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
            [Estimate enc.]
            Est. of Appropriations.
