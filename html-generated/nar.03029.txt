            April 24, 1871.
            Hon. W. W. Belknap,
            Secretary of War.
            Sir:
            In pursuance of the request in your letter of the 18th instant, the District Attorney of West Virginia has been directed to apply to the U.S. District Court for that District for a
            case of cemetery lands, W. Va.
            
            
            
            revision of the decree confirming the appraisement of the land appropriated for a National Cemetery at Grafton.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            see Ins. B'k B. p. 409.
