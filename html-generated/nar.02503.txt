            Oct. 7, 1871.
            Hon. J. H. Howe, Chief Justice,
            Cheyenne, W. T.
            Sir:
            Your resignation was laid before the President this morning, and it has been accepted by him, to take effect on the 31st instant.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l
            &amp; Acting Attorney General.
