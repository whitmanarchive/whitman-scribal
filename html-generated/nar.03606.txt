            Nov. 4, 1871
            Hon. Joseph Segar,
            Ebbitt House, Washington.
            Sir:
                I herewith send you the copy which you desired of the pardon application of Geroge Booker, of Elizabeth Cith County.
                 Very respectfully,
            A.J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
