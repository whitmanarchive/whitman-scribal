           May 5, 1871.
            W. Shafer, Esq.
            Attorney at Law,
            Pittsburgh, Penn.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 15th ultimo in reference to the case of Abraham Shafer, has been received.
         The circumstances to which you refer with regret will not affect my action in the matter.   
       Very respectfully,     
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            Case of A. Shafer W. Penn.
