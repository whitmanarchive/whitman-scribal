           Feb. 18, 1869.
            Hon. Hugh McCulloch,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to enclose a a declaration &amp; agreement on the part of the Union Pacific RR. Co. duly executed by its officers, under authority from the Company, embodying the arrangement upon which a deposit of the first mortgage bonds of the Company was required from it as a guarantee for the completion of its road, its structure and equipments, as required by law. This instrument is in the form required &amp; approved by me.
            I have the honor to be, &amp;c. &amp;c. &amp;c
            Wm. M. Evarts,
            Attorney Gen.
            
            Union Pacific RR Co.
