            Nov. 14, 1871.
            R. M. Sherman, Esq.
            Morgantown, N. C.
            Sir:
            By direction of the Attorney General, I enclose herewith a copy of a letter addressed to the Second Auditor of the Treasury by John Foster, relative to the matter of Capt. Bryson's Company.
            Very respectfully,
             A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
