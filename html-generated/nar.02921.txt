      Feb. 25, 1871.
      Hon. J. F. Asper,
      House of Representatives,
      Sir:
      I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that the United States Attorney for
        the Western District of Missouri has been requested to continue the case of The United
        States vs. H. B. Branch, to the next term of the court.
      case in Missouri continued
      Very respectfully, &amp;c.
      A. J. Falls,
      Chief Clerk.
