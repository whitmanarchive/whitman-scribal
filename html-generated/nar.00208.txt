           April 13th, 1867
Hon. O. H. Browning,
Secretary of the Interior
Sir:
I have this day approved the adjudication of the Commissioner of the General Land Office for the confirmation of certain suspended land entries described in his letter of March 2d, 1867,—and have the honor herewith to return the papers.
Yours very respectfully
Henry Stanbery
Attorney General.
