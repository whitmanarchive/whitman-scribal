            Attorney General's Office.
            August 10, 1865.
            To the
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I have the letter of Mr. G. W. Jones to you of date July 31st 1865 before me.
            Your circular of 29th May 1865 is so explicit that I cannot see that you can do more than to send to Mr. Jones several copies thereof.
            Herewith I send Mr. Jones' letter, &amp; three circulars.
            I have the honor to remain, &amp;c.
            James Speed,
            Attorney General.
