      June 19, 1867.
      Hon. H. McCulloch,
      Secretary of the Treasury.
      Sir:
      I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasury of the United
        States in favor of S. M. McKean, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of
        Twenty four hundred and eighty-eight Dollars, and twenty-four cents, ($2,488:24,) and charge
        the same to the appropriations indicated below.
      I am, with great respect,
      signed
      Henry Stanbery,
      Attorney General.
