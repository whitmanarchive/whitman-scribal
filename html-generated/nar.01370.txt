           June 14, 1870.
            S. M. Davis, Esq.
            Chicago, Ill.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 9th inst. has been received. Whenever an opinion is rendered upon the question about which you inquire—which, as yet, is not done—you may ascertain what it is by corresponding with the Secretary of the Treasury.
            Very respectfully,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
