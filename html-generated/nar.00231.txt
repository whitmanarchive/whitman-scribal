           July 5, 1867.
            Hon. J. F. Wilson,
            Chairman Judiciary Com. H. of R.
            Sir:
            The Attorney General, at the request of the Hon. James Speed, directs me to transmit you the enclosed copies of letters from this office to various U. S. Attorneys, relative to proceedings in confiscation cases.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Clerk.
