            October 20, 1869.
            Messrs. T. &amp; J. W. Johnson &amp; Co.
            Philadelphia.
            Gentlemen:
            I enclose herewith a check for $372:25, the amount of your bill for books this day received.
            I desire to call your attention to the fact that in your schedule of prices furnished this Office the Georgia Reports were put at $5:75 per volume, while in your bill they are charged $6:50. I presume you will correct this in your next bill.
            Very respectfully yours,
            M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
            Library Books.
