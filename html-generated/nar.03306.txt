            Aug. 29, 1871.
            Hon. J. A. J. Creswell,
            Postmaster General.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to return the account of William H. H. Cowles, Esq., for professional services for the P. O. Dept. in North Carolina, transmitted to me in the letter of Mr. Cochran, of the 28th inst. with my certificate, as required by law.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman
            Attorney General.
            P. O. acct.
            N. C.
