            Oct. 16, 1871.
            J. A. Klopp, Esq.
            Middletown, Penn.
            Sir:
            I have received your letter of the 12th instant, and return herewith the papers
                transmitted by you.
            The papers in question have reference to a subject over which this Department has no
                control.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
            no control
