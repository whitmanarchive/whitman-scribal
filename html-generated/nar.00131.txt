           October 9, 1866.
            To
            Hon. O. H. Browning,
            Secretary of the Interior.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit you herewith the account, approved by him, and amounting to Three hundred dollars, ($300.00) of T. Hennessey, for services as assistant to Hon. L. H. Chandler, U. S. Attorney for the Dist. of Virginia, from July 1, 1866, to October 1, 1866,—&amp; to request its payment out of the appropriate fund.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Act'g. Ch'f. Clerk.
