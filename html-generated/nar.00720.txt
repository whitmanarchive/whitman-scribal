            December 1, 1868.
            Hon. W. F. Turner,
            Chief Justice Supreme Court,
            Prescott, Arizona T.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to say that you are hereby granted a leave of absence from your judicial District for three months from January 1, 1869, agreeably to your request of October 16, 1868.
            Very respectfully yours,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
            Leave of absence.
