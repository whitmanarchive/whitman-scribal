            February 27, 1868.
            D. W. Middleton, Esq.
            Clerk Supreme Court U. S.
            Sir:
            I have to request, for the use of this office, a copy of the Opinion of the Court in the case of United States, vs. Julius B. Hartwell, No, 287, present term.
            Respectfully,
            your obedient serv't,
            John M. Binckley,
            Attorney General.
