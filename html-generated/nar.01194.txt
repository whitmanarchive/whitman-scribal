            February 8, 1870.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            In compliance with the request contained in your letter of the 7th inst. I have the honor to transmit herewith a list of all persons in the District of Columbia commissioned by your Department on the requisition of the Attorney General.
            Very respectfully,
            Your obedient servant,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
            trans. certain lists.
