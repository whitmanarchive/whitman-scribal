           August 25, 1868.
            John D. Defrees, Esq.
            Superintendent Public Printing.
            Sir:
            You will please cause to be printed, for the use of this Office, two hundred, (200,) copies of a Circular letter of Instruction to United States Dist. Attorneys . . . a copy of which is herewith enclosed. Please send proof to this office immediately.
            Very respectfully &amp;c. yours,
            J. Hubley Ashton,
            Acting Attorney General
            [copy of circular enclosed.]
