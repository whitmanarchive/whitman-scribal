            Nov. 4, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            Referring to your letter of the 5th of September last, enclosing a copy of a communication addressed to you by J. Hubley Ashton, relative to matters before the Commission of the United States and Mexico, I now have the honor to enclose the affidavit of Don Manuel Castro, obtained as requested by Mr. Ashton, through the Attorney of the U.S. for the Dist. of California, which I will thank you to casecause to be sent to him.
            Very resp'lly,
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l
            &amp; Acting Attorney Gen'l.
            affidavit for Mr. Ashton
