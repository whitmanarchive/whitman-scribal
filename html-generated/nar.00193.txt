            January 26, 1867.
            Hon. Hugh McCulloch,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney Gen'l. to transmit to you the enclosed petition of Charles and Nathaniel
                Friend, and George Thurston—as the case comes under the remitting power of the Secretary of the
                Treasury.
            Very Respectfully, &amp;c.
            F. U. Stitt,
            Pardon Clerk.
