            April 21, 1870.
            Hon. Reverdy Johnson
            Baltimore, Md.
            Sir:
            Agreeably to your request I send you an Opinion of the Attorney General, given April 27, 1869, relating to the compensation of Collectors, naval officers, and surveyors of customs. I have obtained the consent of the Secretary of the Treasury that this opinion may be sent you.
            I have the honor to be,
            Very respectfully,
            W. A. Field,
            Assistant Attorney General.
            trans. copy of opinion.
