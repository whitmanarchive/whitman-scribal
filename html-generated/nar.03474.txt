            Nov. 14, 1871.
            Charles C. Beaman, Jr. Esq.
            Examiner of Claims at Dept. of State.
            Sir:
            In compliance with your letter of the 9th instant, I enclose herewith a copy of the Opinion rendered by Acting Attorney General Talbot, on the 31st of August, 1870, relative to the protection by the United States of vessels purchased by a citizen of a foreign power, &amp;c.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            
            Opinion sent
