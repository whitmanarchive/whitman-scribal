           July 16, 1868.
            Wm. M. Evarts, Esq.
            Windsor, Vermont.
            You have been confirmed. When will you be here? Come immediately, if possible. Appointments must be made, and office organized, before Congress adjourns. There is no time to lose. Day is Surveyor General.
            OH Browning.
            Answer.
            
            Telegram.
