           July 30, 1869.
           Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State,
            Sir:
            I have the honor to enclose to you some accounts received from the Marshal of the Southern District of New York, together with a letter from him directed to this Department. I believe no other action is required respecting them by this Department, and I would ask you to be so kind as to return the letter when you have read it—taking a copy of it, of course, if you
                
                Certain Acct's. U. S. Marshal New York
                
            
            desire to do so.
            Very Respectfully, &amp;c. &amp;c.
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
