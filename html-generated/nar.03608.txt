      Dec. 14, 1871.
      Hon. Wm. Marvin,
      Skaneateles, N. Y.
      Sir: I have received your letter of the 10th inst. and thank you for the information and
        suggestions in the same.
      Very respectfully,
      A. T. Akerman,
      Attorney General.
