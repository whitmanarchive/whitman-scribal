           March 28, 1868.
            Messrs. Thaxter &amp; Bartlett,
            Counsellors at Law,
            Boston, Mass.
            Shall case of steamer Siren be continued? Will be reached on last day of the term. The Attorney General cannot attend in Court. I do not wish to argue without him.
            J. Hubley Ashton
            Special Counsel of U. S.
