            Oct. 12, 1871.
            Gen. E. D. Townsend,
            Adjutant General U.S. A.
            Sir:
            I hand you herewith a telegram addressed to the Attorney General at Yorkville, S. C., which I will thank you to have sent to him in cipher as soon as possible.
            Very respectfully,
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l
            &amp; Acting Attorney Gen'l.
            send telegram to Atty Gen'l
