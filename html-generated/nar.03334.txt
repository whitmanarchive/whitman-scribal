            Sept. 12, 1871.
            Alexander Johnston, Esq.
            Assistant Attorney Gen's office, Court of Claims
            Sir:
            Will you report where upon the docket stands the case of Case and Van Wagenen, vs. The United States—and what is the condition of preparation in that case?
            Very respectfully, &amp;c
            A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
            Sup. Ct. Case
