            Nov. 21, 1871.
            J. O. Glover, Esq.
            U. S. Attorney,
            Chicago, Ill.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 13th instant, and in compliance with your request to enclose herewith a copy of the petition of the Illinois Central RR. Company, which was left at this office by the Solicitor of said Railroad some time since.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
            certain copy sent
