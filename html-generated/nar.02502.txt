            Oct. 7, 1871.
            A. G. Brandner, Esq.
            U. S. Commissioner,
            Alba City, U. T.
            Sir:
            This Department has received your letter of the 29th ultimo, wherein you ask to be furnished with a copy of "Brightly's Digest and Appendix." In reply, I have to inform you that such books are not furnished
            
            cannot furnish books
            
            
            
            
             to U. S. Commissioners by this, or any other Executive Departments of the Government.
            I regret that it is not in my power to comply with your request.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk
