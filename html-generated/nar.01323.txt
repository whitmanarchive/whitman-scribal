            April 15, 1870.
            General J. W. Denver,
            Care of Hughes, Denver &amp; Peck,
            Washington, D. C.
            General,
            I am directed by the Attorney General to notify you that the time for furnishing evidence to him in the land patent case of N. S. Murphy, of California,—which, by letter from this office of 2d inst. was limited to the 18th inst.—is now further extended to the 25th inst.
            Very respectfully,
            M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
            case of N. S. Murphy Cal.
                see p. 258, ante
