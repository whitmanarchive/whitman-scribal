            Oct. 18, 1871.
            Gen. E. D. Townsend,
            Adjutant General U.S. A.
            Sir:
            I hand you herewith a dispatch addressed to the Attorney General at Yorkville, S. C., which I will thank you to have transmitted to him in cipher with as little delay as possible.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General,
            &amp; Acting Attorney General.
            
            send Tel. to the Att'y Gen'l
