            June 9, 1869.
            Hon. H. Van Aernam,
            Commissioner of Pensions.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of this date, requesting a list of the names and residences of the various officers of the Circuit and District Courts of the U. S.—and to say, in reply, that a list of such officers of those Courts as are under the supervision of this office, has been furnished to the Secretary of the Interior, to whom you are respectfully referred.
            Very Resp. yours,
            W. A. Field,
            Ass't. Attorney Gen'l.
