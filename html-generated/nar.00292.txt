            November 12, 1867.
            Hon. N. L. Jeffries,
            Register of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            By direction of the Attorney General, I transmit the estimate of appropriations required for the service of this Office, for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1869.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c. &amp;c.
            F. U. Stitt,
            Acting Chief Clerk.
