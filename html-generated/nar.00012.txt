            Attorney General's Office.
            August 3d, 1865.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the President to request that you cause a Commission to issue to Edward C. Carrington, to be United States Attorney for the District of Columbia, to date August 3, 1865.
            I am sir, very respectfully,
            Your obedient servant,
            J. Hubley Ashton,
            Acting Attorney General
            
            Hon. W. Hunter,
            Acting Secretary of State
