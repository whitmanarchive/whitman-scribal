            Nov. 7, 1871.
            Hon. J. W. Fisher,
            Chief Justice,
            Wyoming Terr.
            Sir:
          I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 1st inst. transmitting a copy of your official oath, and referring to a mistake made in your Christian name in your Commission.
            If you will return the Commission to me, I will have the proper correction made.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
         
            Judge Fisher's Commission
