           March 3, 1869.
            To the President.
           Sir:
            I beg leave respectfully to resign the office of Attorney General of the United States, such resignation to take effect with the expiration of the present Presidential term.
            I have the honor to be,
            with great respect,
            your ob't serv't.
            Wm. M. Evarts.
