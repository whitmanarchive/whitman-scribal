            Dec. 28, 1871.
            James A. Morgan,
            Washington, D. C.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 27th instant, and to inform you that the papers to which you refer are not on the files of this Department. The paper enclosed in your letter is herewith returned.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
