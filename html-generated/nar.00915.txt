           May 11, 1869
            Thos. J. Durant, Esq.
            Washington, D. C.
            Sir:
            The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 10th inst. relative to the case of the United States vs. 6 Lots of ground, property of T. J. Semmes; and to say that he has forwarded to the U. S. Attorney at New Orleans instructions in the case, a copy of which will be furnished to you on your application to him.
            Very Resp. yours,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Clerk.
            
            U. S. ag't. 6 Lots of land (T. J. Semmes, Louisiana)
