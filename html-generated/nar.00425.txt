            February 1, 1868.
            D. W. Middleton, Esq.
            Clerk Supreme Court United States.
            Sir:
            I learn that a case from the Southern District of New York, entitled Hiram Barney, Collector, plaintiff in error, vs. C. F. Schneider, et al., has been docketed No. 416, December term, 1867.
            We would thank you for a copy of the record as early as practicable.
            Yours respectfully,
            John M. Binckley,
            Assistant Attorney General.
            
            p. 78
            Ins. Book p. 19
