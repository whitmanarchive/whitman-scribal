            Dec. 27, 1871.
            James Neville, Esq.
            Omaha, Nebraska.
            Sir:
            I enclose herewith a commission appointing you to the office of Attorney of the United States for the District of Nebraska.
            If the same is accepted I will thank you to transmit to this Department a copy of your official oath, and to inform me of the place of your nativity.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
