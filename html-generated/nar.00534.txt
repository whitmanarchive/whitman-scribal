            May 14, 1868.
            R. J. Atkinson, Esq.
            Present.
            Sir:
            By direction of the Attorney General ad interim, I transmit you herewith a certified copy of the pardon papers in the case of E. E. Simpson.
            Respectfully, &amp;c.
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
