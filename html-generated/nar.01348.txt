            May 23, 1870.
            Hon. Geo. M. Robeson,
            Secretary of the Navy.
            Sir:
            I send enclosed herewith the papers accompanying your letter of the 20th ult. relating to the case of Wm. Pollard, as I am informed that you do not now desire an opinion upon the question stated in that letter
            Very Resp. your obd't serv't,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
            returning papers.
