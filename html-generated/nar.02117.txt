            Nov. 3, 1871.
            L. D. Latimer, Esq.
            U. S. Attorney,
            San Francisco, Cal.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that your account for services in the matter of the investigation before the Surveyor General, touching the swamp and overflowed lands in California, has been approved, and referred to the accounting officers of the Treasury for payment.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
                
            acc't approved
            Cal.
