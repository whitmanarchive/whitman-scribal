           July 19, 1869.
          Thos. L. Smith, Esq.
           First Auditor of the Treasury.
           Sir:
            The U. S. Attorney has sent to this Office the original bills for taking depositions in U. S. vs. Heard, and other cases, which are herewith transmitted to you pursuant to your request.
            Very Respectfully,
            W. F. Peddrick,
            Acting Chief Clerk.         
            
            Bills of U. S. Attorney
