           April 21, 1870
           Hon. Reverdy Johnson
           Baltimore, Md.
           Sir:
            Agreeably to your request I send you an Opinion of the Attorney General, given April 27, 1869, relating to the compensation of Collectors, naval officers, and surveyors of customs. I have obtained the consent of the Secretary of the Treasury that this Opinion may be sent you. trans. copy of opinion
           I have the honor to be,
           Very respectfully,
           W. A. Field.
           Assistant Attorney General.
