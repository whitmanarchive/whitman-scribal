            Oct. 25, 1866
            To
            Hon. Hugh McCulloch,
            Sec. of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to enclose herewith a copy of a letter requesting me to sanction a motion to the Supreme Court for the early argument of certain private causes in which your Department may have an interest.
            I will be happy to receive any suggestions you may desire to offer on the subject:matter of the enclosed communication.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c
            Henry Stanbery,
            Attorney General.
