            Attorney General's Office.
            August 22, 1865.
            Jno. K. Goodloe, Esq.
            Louisville, Ky.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit herewith a Commission appointing you to be Attorney of the United States for the Eas. Dist. of Louisiana; and to request that you send, along with a copy of your official oath, a statement of the place of your nativity for the Biennial Register. I am also directed by the Attorney General to request that you will await a letter from him, which he will write at his earliest leisure.
            I am sir, very Resp. your obd't serv't
            Wm. Stewart, Chief Clerk.
            [Com. enc. with above.]
