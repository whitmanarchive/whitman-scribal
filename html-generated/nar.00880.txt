            April 26, 1869.
            The President.
            Sir:
            I enclose the form of an Executive order suspending Isaac N. Quimby as Marshal of the Northern District of New York, which I would advise you to adopt.
            Very resp. yours, &amp;c. &amp;c.
            E. R. Hoar
            Attorney General.
