           Nov. 7, 1871,
           L. D. Latimer, Esq.
           U. S. Attorney,
           San Francisco, Cal.
           Sir:
           In reply to your letter of the 30th ultimo, I have to inform you that your account for services rendered on behalf of the United States before the Surveyor General of California, touching Swamp and Overflowed  lands, &amp;c., was approved by the Attorney General on the 3d instant, of which approval I advised you on the same date.
            
            Very respectfully,
           A. J. Falls,
           Chief Clerk.
           acc't approved.
