            December 20, 1870.
            Hon. Geo. Boutwell,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 19th inst., and to inform you that I have directed the Attorney of the U. S. at St. Louis to at once comply with the regulations of your Department by furnishing a list of the furniture now in his office, to the Surveyor of customs.
            Very respectfully &amp;c
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General
            and Acting Attorney General.
            letters &amp;c. acknowledged
