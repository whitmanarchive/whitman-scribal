            November 13, 1867.
            Hon. George W. McClellan,
            Second Assistant Postmaster General.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt this day of your letter of 12th inst. transmitting a post-route map of the New England States, and to express his acknowledgements therefor.
            I have the honor to be,
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            F. U. Stitt,
            Acting Chief Clerk.
