           October 23, 1866.
            To
            Hiram Willey, Esq.
            U. S. Attorney,
            New London, Conn.
            Sir:
            You are instructed, by request of the Secretary of War, to defend any suit brought in a State Court, or otherwise, against Capt. &amp; Bv't. Maj. W. R. Pease, U. S.A. Chief Must'gMustering &amp;  Disb. Officer for Conn. &amp; R. J., at Hartford, by one B. F. Ellis, for damages to certain premises, mentioned in the enclosed letter, to the Adj. Gen.
            Yours respectfully,
            J. Hubley Ashton,
            Ass't. Att'y. Gen'l.
