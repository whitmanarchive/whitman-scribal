      March 6, 1871.
      Hon. Columbus Delano,
      Secretary of the Interior.
      Sir:
      The U.S. Attorney for the District of Columbia has been directed to appear for the
        defence in the suit brought by John B. Wright against John N. Craig, which is the subject of
        your letters of the 25th ultimo, and 4th instant. I advise that Captain Craig enter into
        personal communication with the District Attorney on the subject.
      defense of J. N. Craig, Dist. Col.
      Very respectfully, &amp;c.
      A. T. Akerman,
      Attorney General.
