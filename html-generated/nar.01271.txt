           March 24, 1870
           To Clerk U. S. Circuit Court
           Albany, N. Y.
           In the suit of David M. Osborne, and another against Joseph Gates, assessor, &amp;c., now in United States Supreme Court, on filing the declaration was there any praecipe or summons or process issued by the Circuit Court, and served upon the defendant? answer by telegraph.
          W. A. Field, 
           Assistant Attorney General.
            Tel. 
            certain suit
