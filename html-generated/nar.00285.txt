            November 9, 1867
            Telegram.
            Wm. M. Evarts, Esq.
            Counsellor at Law,
            New York City.
            Mr. Chandler wishes to have a consultation as to Davis' case, in the office—next Wednesday morning—will expect you.
            Henry Stanbery
            Attorney General.
