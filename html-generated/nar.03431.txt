            Oct. 30, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the President to request you to issue a commission to take effect on the 1st promixo, appointing Charles C. Beaman, Jr. to the office of Examiner of Claims, in place of E. Peshine Smith, Esq., resigned.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
