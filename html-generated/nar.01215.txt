           February 19, 1870.
            Hon. G. G. Symes,
            Asso. Justice, Montana Terr.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney General to say that the leave of absence heretofore granted to you is extended hereby to March 1st, 1870.
            Very respectfully yours,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
            
            Leave of absence.
