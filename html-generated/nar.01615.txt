            January 10, 1871.
            Messrs. J. Hartman and others,
            San Francisco, California.
            Sir:
            In answer to yours of the 18th of November last, I have the honor to say that this Department, as at present advised, does not intend to move to reinstate the appeal in
                case of U. S. vs. Olvera (Cal.)
                
                the case of the United States, vs. Olvera, Cuyamaca case.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
