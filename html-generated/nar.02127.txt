            Nov. 7, 1871
            Hon. H. H. Wells, U. S. Attorney,
            Richmond, Va.
            Sir:  I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 6th instant, and, in compliance with your request to enclose herewith a copy of the circular issued under date of March 2d,  by
                Attorney General to the District Attorneys of the United States relative to the Amnesty Proclamation.
            Very resp'lly,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
            
            Circular sent.
