            June 10, 1868.
            Hon. C. C. Hewitt,
            Chief Justice Supreme Court,
            Washington Terr.
            Sir:
            The Attorney General ad interim directs me to say that the leave of absence heretofore granted you is hereby extended seventy five days, in accordance with the request, and for the reasons specified in the application of the Hon. Alvan Flanders, of the 9th instant.
            Very Respectfully, your obedient servant,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
