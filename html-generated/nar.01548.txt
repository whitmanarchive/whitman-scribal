           December 16, 1870.
            Hon. A. McDonald,
            U. S. Senate.
            Dear Sir:
            I have been directed by the Attorney General to inform you that the opinion in the case of the Choctaw bonds was sent this morning to the Secretary of the Treasury.
            Very resp'y. your ob't servt,
            A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
