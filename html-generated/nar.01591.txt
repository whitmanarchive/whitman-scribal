            January 3, 1871.
            Hon. Amos T. Akerman,
            Kimball House,
            Atlanta, Georgia.
            Since my dispatch of last night, I have seen the President, who directs me to say to you that your immediate presence in Washington is not necessary.
            B. H. Bristow,
            Solicitor General.
            Tel.
            official
