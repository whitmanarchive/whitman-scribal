            January 7, 
                1867
                1868
            .
            Hon. O. H. Browning,
            Secretary of the Interior.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to Three hundred and seventy five dollars, ($375:00,) of M. L. Perkins for services as assistant to S. L. Warren, Esq. United States Attorney for the Western District of Tennessee, for the quarter ending December 31st 1867; and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund.
            Very Respectfully, &amp;c.
            Henry Stanbery,
            Attorney General.
