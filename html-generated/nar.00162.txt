            Oct. 30, 1866.
            To
            Gen. J. W. Denver,
            Sir:
            I have rec'd yours of this date, with enclosures as stated.
            I enclose herewith:
            1st; Senator McDougal's letter of 8th inst.
            2d; McClosky's note of 23d inst.
            3d; Mr. Leese's recommendations for the office of Marshal.
            4th; My acceptance of Mr. Leese's resignation
            Very respectfully
            Henry Stanbery.
