            Nov. 13, 1871.
            Lewis E. Johnson, Esq. U. S. Marshal,
            Columbia, S. C.
            Sir:
            The letter of Wm Magill Fleming, Esq. of Nov. 7th, with your
            
            
            
            
           
            postscript, in relation to the case of Benjamin F. Bates, has been received. The matter has been referred to the District Attorney of South Carolina, with instructions to release Mr. Bates on bail if the statements of Mr. Fleming shall be ascertained to be correct.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            B. F. Bates
            S. C.
