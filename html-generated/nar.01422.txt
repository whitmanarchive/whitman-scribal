            July 20, 1870.
            F. A. Simkins, Esq.
            Washington, D. C.
            Dear Sir:
            In answer to your application for a position as clerk in this Department, dated the 8th inst. I have to inform you that the clerkships under my control have either been filled or promised.
         Very respectfully,
A. T. Akerman,
Attorney General.
            Official reply &amp;c. 
            David G. Barnitz, Wash. D. C.; Edwin Frey, Charleston, W. Va} duplicates of above
