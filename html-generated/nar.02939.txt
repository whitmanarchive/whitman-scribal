      March 6, 1871.
      Hon. Hamilton Fish,
      Secretary of State.
      Sir:
      I am directed by the President to request you to issue a Commission appointing William
        Story to be U.S. District Judge for the Western District of Arkansas, agreeably to the
        enclosed resolution of the Senate.
      Commission
      Very respectfully,
      A. T. Akerman,
      Attorney General.
