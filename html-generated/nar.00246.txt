           August 30, 1867.
            Chas. H. Brown, Esq.
            93 Water street, Boston, Mass.
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Acting Attorney General to say that the prize cases mentioned in your letter of the 26th inst. have been decided by the Supreme Court, and are now ended.
            Very respectfully,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
