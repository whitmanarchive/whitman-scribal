            Oct. 31, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 30th instant, enclosing therein the receipted account of James P. Newcomb, Esq. for $101, for expenses incurred in obtaining the deposition of Capt. James Speed; and to inform you that a draft for the amount thereof has been sent by this day's mail to Mr. Newcomb, of Austin, Texas.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
            J. P. Newcomb's acc't. $101 paid
