            Aug. 4, 1871.
            Pat. O. Hawes, Esq.
            Omaha, Nebraska.
            I have received your letter of the 28th ultimo, and will endeavour to bear in mind the suggestions which you have made in regard to United States officers.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
