           December 10, 1867.
            D. W. Middleton, Esq.
            Clerk Supreme Court, U. S.
            Sir:
            I herewith file for the United States, for docket in due order and printing, the Record in the case of the United States plaintiffs in Error, vs. Charles Hare, et al, from the District of California.
            Respectfully &amp;c.
            John M. Binckley,
            Ass't. Attorney Gen'l.
