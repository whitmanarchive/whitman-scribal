           January 16, 1871.
           Hon. C. S. Eyster. Asso. Just. Sup. Court.
           Denver. Colorado Terr.
           Sir:
           The leave of absence for fifteen days from the 18th instant, requested in your letter of the 11th, inst. is granted.
            Very res'll'y
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            leave of absence.
