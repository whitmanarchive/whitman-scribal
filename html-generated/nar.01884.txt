            June 1, 1869.
            Hon. Abram Bergen,
            Asso. Justice,
            Santa Fé, New Mex.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 21st ult. enclosing a copy of your official oath, has been received. In view of the circumstances stated, you are hereby granted a leave of absence from July 15th to Oct. 15th. You will please report your return to the Territory to this office.
            Very Resp. yours,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
