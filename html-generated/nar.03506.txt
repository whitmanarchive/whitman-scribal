            Dec. 1, 1871.
            Hon. Hamilton Fish,
            Secretary of State.
            Sir:
            In answer to a letter from Mr. Hunter, Acting Secretary of State, dated the 29th ultimo, inquiring concerning instructions from this Department to the District Attorney of Dakota, I have the honor to enclose a copy of my letter of instructions sent to Warren Cowles, Esq. District Attorney of Dakota, on the 15th ultimo.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            
            case in Dakota
