            October 20, 1866.
            To
            Hon. Hugh McCulloch,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States, in favor of S. M. McKean, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Two thousand and seventy-eight dollars and twenty seven cents, ($2,078:27,) and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            Henry Stanbery,
            Attorney General.
            Salary of the Attorney General for October, 1866...$673:91
            "      "  "   Ass't Att'y Gen'l
            "       "   294.84
            "      "  Clerks and Messenger
                "      "   1109.52
               $2078:27
          
            ☞ The above is cancelled.
