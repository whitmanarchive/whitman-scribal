            July 16, 1869.
            J. R. Boone, Esq.
            Louisville, Kentucky.
            Sir:
            Pursuant to the request contained in your letter of the 2d instant, I am directed by the Attorney General to return the enclosed. I will state at the same time that, after an examination of our files, this is the only paper found relating to the application mentioned.
            Very Respectfully yours,
            W. F. Peddrick,
            Acting Chief Clerk.
            
            paper returned
