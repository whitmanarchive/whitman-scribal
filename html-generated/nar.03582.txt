            Dec. 23, 1871.
            Charles C. Beaman, Esq.
            Sir:
            I enclose herewith a Commission appointing you to the office of Examiner of Claims in the Department of State.
            If the same be accepted, I will thank you to transmit to this Department your official oath, and to inform me of the place of your nativity.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General
