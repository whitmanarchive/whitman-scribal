            April 14, 1869.
            Publisher of "the Nation," New York.
            Sir:
            Enclosed please find check for $5, for one year's subscription to the Nation, from March 4, 1869.
            Very Resp. yours,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Ch. Clerk.
