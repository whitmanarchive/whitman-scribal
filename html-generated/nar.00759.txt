           January 29, 1869.
            Hon. H McCulloch
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a letter just sent to the Dist. Attorney for the Western Dist. of Pennsylvania, relative to the case of Arthur Ballou,—which is the subject of your communication of this date.
            I am sir, &amp;c &amp;c. &amp;c.
          Acting Attorney General
