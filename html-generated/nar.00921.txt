           May 12, 1869.
            Hon. Evariste Laroche,
            Chargé de Affaires, &amp; Consul Gen'l. of Haiti.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letters of the 6th and 11th insts. and to say in reply that I have transmitted them to the Secretary of State, to whom, according to the usages of our Government, all official communications from the Representatives of foreign powers should be addressed.
            Very Resp. Sir, &amp;c. &amp;c.
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
            
            Exp. ag't Haiti.
