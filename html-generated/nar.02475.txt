            Sept. 16, 1871.
            S. S. Kingston, Esq.
            246 North Second street,
            Philadelphia.
            Sir:
            In reply to your letter of the 14th instant, I have to inform you that the vacancy
                to which you refer has been filled.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
            vacancy filled.
