            Feb. 24, 1871.
            Messrs. Tucker &amp; Alvord.
            Gentlemen:
            Upon an examination of the records, it does not appear that the papers in the case of E. G. Bradby, of Cotton Plant, Arkansas, referred to in your letter of the 15th instant, have been filed in this Department.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            no papers.
