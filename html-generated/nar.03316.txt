            Sept. 6, 1871.
            Hon. T. H. Talbot,
            Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
            Sir: You omitted to enclose with your letter of yesterday the account therein referred to of Mr. Bent. Will you transmit it to me at your earliest convenience?
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman, Att'y Gen'l.
