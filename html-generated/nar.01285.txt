           March 30, 1870.
           To Hon. Henry Stanbery,
           Cincinnati, Ohio.
           A case like No. 237, United States against John M. Pugh, has been assigned by the Supreme Court for April 12th. Can you argue this at the same time? Answer by telegraph.
           E. R. Hoar,
           Attorney General
           (charge office)
            Tel.
