            Nov. 14, 1871.
            C. Cochran, Jr. Esq.
            Superintendent of Mail Depredations,
            Post Office Dept.
            Dear Sir:
            I return with my thanks the letter of the Attorney General to the Postmaster General of the 24th of August last, which you were kind enough to transmit to me in your letter of the 13th inst.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk
