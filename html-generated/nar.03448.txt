            Nov. 6, 1871.
            Hon. Wm. McMichael,
            Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
            Dear Sir:
            I enclose herewith a letter addressed to Mr. William W. Marshall, Ass't Att'y Gen'l. If it is not intended for you, will you be kind enough to return it to me at your convenience?
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
