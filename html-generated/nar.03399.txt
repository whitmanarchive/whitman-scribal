            Oct. 17, 1871.
            Hon. Geo. L. Boutwell,
            Secretary of the Treasury.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to hand you herewith, for your information, a copy of a letter dated the 9th instant, written by J. A. H. Clenland, addressed to the Attorney General.
            Very respectfully, &amp;c.
            B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General,
            &amp; Acting Attorney General.
            
            letter sent.
