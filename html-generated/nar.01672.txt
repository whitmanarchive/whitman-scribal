           January 31, 1871.
            Hon. T. H. Talbot,
            Assistant Attorney Gen'l
            Sir:
            I am directed by the Attorney Gen'l to transmit to you the enclosed order of this Department in relation to appearances on the part of the United States, in the Court of Claims.
            Very respectfully,
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
