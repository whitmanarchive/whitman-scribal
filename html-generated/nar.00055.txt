            Attorney General's Office,
            August 30, 1865.
            James Christian, Esq.
            Lawrence, Kansas.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 22d inst. tendering your resignation as U. S. Attorney for the Territory of Dakota, is received,- and your resignation is accepted; though I sincerely regret the existence of the circumstances that induced you to take this step.
            I am sir, very respectfully,
            your obedient servant,
            James Speed
            Attorney General.
