           March 1, 1870.
            Messrs. T. &amp; J. W. Johnson &amp; Co.
            Philadelphia, Pa.
            Gentlemen:
            The books mentioned in your letter of yesterday's date as having been forwarded by express were received to-day.
            You are requested to furnish us with another copy of "Hoffman's Land Cases," provided it can be done at the same price, $10. In regard to the Alabama Reports referred to in a previous letter, we have concluded to let you furnish them also, believing
                
                Library Books 
            
                
                you will do so at as reasonable a figure as it is possible under all the circumstances.
            Very respectfully,
            your obd't serv't,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
