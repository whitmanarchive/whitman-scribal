           June 17, 1870.
            E. M. Clark, Esq.
            Box 89, West Point, Nebraska.
            Sir:
            Your letter of the 8th inst. has been received. The Attorney General directs me to state that the accounts, copies of which are requested by you, are not kept in this Department, but in the Department of the Interior.
            Very respectfully,
            W. F. Peddrick.
            
            letter answered.
