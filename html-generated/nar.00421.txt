            February 3, 1868.
            Hon. Henry Stanbery,
            Attorney General.
            Sir:
            I have the honor to state for your information, that it appears, from the regular record in this office of the dates of Commissions of District Attorneys of the United States, that the office of District Attorney for the District of West Virginia is vacant.
            Very Respectfully, sir, your obd't sv't.
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
