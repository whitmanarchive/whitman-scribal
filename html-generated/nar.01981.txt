            May 25, 1871.
            Hon. J. R. Lewis,
            Boisé City, Idaho.
            Sir:
            I have received from the Department of State a commission appointing you to be an Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the Territory of New Mexico, from the first day of July next.
            I will thank you to let me know your address, and where you wish the commission sent.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
            
            Commission
