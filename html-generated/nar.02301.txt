            Dec. 28, 1871.
            B. F. Fifield, Esq.
            U. S. Attorney,
            Montpelier, Vt.
            Sir:
            Yours of the 26th in relation to the recognizance of F. E. Spalding, is received.
            F. E. Spalding
            Vt.
            
            
            
            I see no reason for interfering with the regular course of law in the matter.
            Very respectfully,
            A. T. Akerman,
            Attorney General.
