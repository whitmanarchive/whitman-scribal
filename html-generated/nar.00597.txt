            July 11, 1868.
            Messrs. Little, Brown, &amp; Co.
            110 Washington street,
            Boston,   Mass.
            Gentlemen:
            Please find enclosed a check for $19:25, the amount of your bill as rendered to date.
            Very Respectfully Yours,
            M. F. Pleasants,
            Chief Clerk.
