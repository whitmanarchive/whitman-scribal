           August 31, 1868.
           Messrs. Evarts, Southmayd, &amp; Choate,
           No. 52 Wall Street,
            New York City.
            Gentlemen:
            I am in receipt of your letter of the 28th inst, &amp; enclose herewith proof sheets of the opinion to which I suppose you refer. If it will be convenient for you to return them, I will be obliged.
            Very truly yours,
            J. Hubley Ashton,
            Acting Att'y. Gen.
