           January 24, 1868.
            Hon. A. W. Randall,
            Postmaster General.
            Sir:
            I herewith enclose, for your information, a copy of instructions just sent from this office in a case of alleged criminal practices on the part of an officer of your Department.
            Very Respectfully
            Your obedient servant,
            John M. Binckley
            Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
            
            See Ins. book p 15
