            April 12, 1869.
            Hon. J. D. Cox,
            Secretary of the Interior.
            Sir:
            In reply to your letter of the 19th inst. touching the question
            
            tr to Opinion Book 
            
            
            
            of the eligibility of Gen. E. S. Parker, to the office of Chief of a Bureau of your Department, I have the honor to state that, on the facts presented, I do not perceive that he is disqualified from holding such office, under the Constitution and laws of the United States.
            I am sir, with great respect,
            Your obedient servant,
            E. R. Hoar,
            Attorney General.
