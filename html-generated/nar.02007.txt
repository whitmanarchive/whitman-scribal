            June 6, 1871.
            W. C. Riley, Esq.
            Eastville, Va.
            Sir:          
            In reply to your communication of the 1st instant. I have the
                
            
            
            honor to inform you that Mr. B. W. Gray was not confirmed as U. S. Judge for the Eastern District of Texas.
            
            Very respectfully, &amp;c
            A. J. Falls,
            Chief Clerk.
            
            non-confirmed
