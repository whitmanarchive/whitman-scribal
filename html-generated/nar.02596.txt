      Nov. 17, 1871.
      Hon. Joseph W. Fisher,
      Chief Justice of Wyoming Terr.
      Cheyenne.
      Dear Sir: I enclose herewith your commission as Chief Justice, with correction in
        Christian name.
      Very respectfully,
      A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
