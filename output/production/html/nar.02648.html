Dec. 6, 1871. R. B. Rutherford, Esq. Mattoon, Illinois. Sir: This Department has no control over the matter of soldiers' discharges, whic​ h​ is the subject of your letter of the 30th ultimo,—and I suggest that your application be made to the War Department. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
