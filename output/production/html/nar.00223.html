June 17, 1867. Hon. W. T. Otto, Acting Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit, herewith, the account, approved by me, & amounting to Sixty dollars & twenty-six cents ($60.26,) of J. Sterling Smith, for services as assistant U. S. Attorney for the Northern District of New York; & to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very Respectfully, &c signedHenry Stanbery Attorney General.
