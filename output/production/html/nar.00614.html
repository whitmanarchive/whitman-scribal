July 25, 1868. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. Gentlemen: The bill which I return herewith is correct. Please receipt it, and send a duplicate,—when a check will be forwarded to you. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
