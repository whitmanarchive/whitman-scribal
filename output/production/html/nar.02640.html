Dec. 4, 1871. Major Lewis Merrill, U.S. A. Yorkville, S. C. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 29th ultimo, enclosing warrants for the arrest of James Wm. Avery, now a fugitive from justice. These papers shall receive prompt attention. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
