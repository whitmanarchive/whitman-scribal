December 31, 1869. Jared B. Flagg, Esq. New Haven, Conn. Sir: The Express Company have just delivered the Portrait of Mr. Stanbery. It is in perfect order. Please find enclosed a check on the Assistant Treasurer at New York for Eight Hundred Dollars, ($800:) Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk. (check enc.)
