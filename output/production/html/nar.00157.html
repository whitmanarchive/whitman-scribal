Oct. 25, 1866. To W. A. Dart, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Potsdam, N. Y. Sir: Your letter of the 22d inst. relative to the case of the U. S. vs. Swift & Courtney has been received, &, with its enclosure, referred to the Dist Attorney for the Southern Dist. of New York. Very respectfully &c/ M. F. Pleasants, Act'g Ch. Clerk.
