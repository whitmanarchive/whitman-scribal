Nov. 2, 1871. Hon. Geo. C. Bates, Chicago, Ill. Sir: I enclose herewith a Commission appointing you to the office of Attorney of the United States for Utah Territory. If the same is accepted, I will thank you to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department, and to inform me of the place of your nativity. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
Commission U. S. Att'y Utah
