December 30, 1867. Hon. J. P. Kidder, Associate Justice, Vermilion, Dakota Terr. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence for two months, from February 1st, 1868, in accordance with the request contained in your letter of the 23d instant. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
