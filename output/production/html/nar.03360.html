Sept. 29, 1871. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Dear Sir: I have this morning received a dispatch from District Attorney Davis of New York in which he advises me that in his opinion the conviction of Hodge of a felony, by court martial, will not render him incompetent to testify against Polhamus​ and Jackson. Very resp'lly, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l, & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
Hodge.
