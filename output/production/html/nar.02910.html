April 15, 1871. Hon. J. G. Palen, Chief Justice, &c. Elizabethtown, Colfax Co. New Mexico. Sir: I have received your letter of the 3d instant, repeating your request for a leave of absence. On the 22d of March last, I answered your former letter in a communication, of which I enclose a copy. The decision therein expressed is adhered to. The action of the Department is in accordance with the Executive order, a copy of which I enclose. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Leave declined
