June 5, 1868. M. Anker, Esq. Pueblo, Colorado Terr. Sir: In response to your communication of 28th ultimo, I enclose a copy of ours of this date to the United States Attorney for the Territory of Colorado. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General
