Dec. 19, 1871. George C. Bates, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Salt Lake City, Utah. Employ Baskin—compensation to be determined by the Department. Make no agreements as to bail, while Young absconds. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Tel. (Spe. Counsel)
