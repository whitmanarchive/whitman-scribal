January 8, 1868. E. W. Andrews, Esq. Attorney at Law, Harper's Ferry, West Va. Sir: Referring to your letter of 23d ultimo, relative to certain ejectment suits, I now enclose for your information copies of a report of the chief of ordnance War Department, and of the letter of the Secretary of War, adinterim, on the subject, dated 4th instant. Very Respectfully, your ob'dt servant Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
