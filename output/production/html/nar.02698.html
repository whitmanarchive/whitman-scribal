Dec. 28, 1871. Mr. Thomas M. Peters, Moulton, Ala. Sir: I received your letter of the 11th instant, and thank you for the information therein conveyed. Your suggestions shall receive attention. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
