November 18, 1868. A. H. Garland, Esq. Little Rock, Ark. Dear Sir: Your brief, in case of Gaines v. Thompson, was received. This office will take charge of the case, and will make such use of your brief as may be necessary.— It seems to cover the point involved. Yours Respectfully, J. Hubley Ashton, Acting Attorney General
