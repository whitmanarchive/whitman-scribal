April 7, 1871. Hon. O.F. Strickland, Asso. Justice of Utah Territory. St. James Hotel, Washington, D. C. Sir: In pursuance of the request in your letter of this date, the leave of absence granted to you for the month of June next in my letter of the 11th ult. is revoked, and leave of absence from your Territory is granted to you for the current month, April, 1871. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
