February 29, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court U. S. Sir: On the 27th instant, I requested one copy of the Opinion of the Court in the case of the United States, vs. Hartwell. I have to request now an additional copy—both, as soon as the Opinion is printed. Respectfully, &c. yours, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
