Dec. 14, 1871. Hon. Noah Davis, U. S. Attorney, Sir: Your letter of the 12th inst. with the enclosures in relation to the cases of Jordan and Ryan, has been received. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Att'y Gen'l.
Jordan & Ryan.
