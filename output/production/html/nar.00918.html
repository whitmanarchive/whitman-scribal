May 12, 1869. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a letter of the 11th inst. from Mr. Evariste Laroche, Chargé d'Affaires of Hayti, in relation to the steamer "Florida." Very Respectfully, &c. E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
Exp. ag't Haiti
