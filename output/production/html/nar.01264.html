March 22, 1870. Samuel Bowles, Esq. Springfield, Mass. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General I transmit herewith ten dollars for one year's subscription of this office to the Springfield Republican. I will thank you to send me a receipt in duplicate. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
