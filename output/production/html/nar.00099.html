Attorney General's Office, April 11, 1866. To Bennett Pike, Esq. U. S. Attorney Western Dist. Mo. St. Josephs, Missouri Sir: You are hereby instructed to dismiss the proceedings for confiscation against John K. Dougherty decea'd, upon the payment of costs. I am sir, very respectfully, James Speed, Attorney General.
