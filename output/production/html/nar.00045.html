Attorney General's Office. August 22, 1865. Darius U. Starbuck​ , Esq. Salem, North Carolina. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit herewith a Commission appointing you to be U. S. Attorney for the District of North Carolina; and to request that you will send, along with a copy of your official oath, a statement of the place of your birth for the Biennial Register. I am sir, very respectfully, Your obedient servant Wm Stewart Chief Clerk. [Commission sent with above.]
