November 7, 1867. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to Three Hundred and seventy-five Dollars, ($375.00,) of Marland L. Perkins, for services as Assistant United States Attorney for the District of Western Tennessee, and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
