Feb. 28, 1871. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chairman of Judiciary Committee, U.S. Senate. Sir: I send you a copy of a letter received at this office from the Chief Justice and several other officials for the Territory of Utah—and commend the subject of it to the attention of your committee Very respectfully, your obd't serv't, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
