March 24, 1869. Hon. Geo. L. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a number of papers respecting the settlement of the acc't of Jos. Laughlin, late a lieutenant of Volunteers, accompanied by a letter from him of the 18th inst., in which it has been alleged that a fraud has been committed by his agent upon the government,—and to suggest that they be referred to the Second Comptroller for investigation. Very Resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
