Nov. 9, 1866 (Telegram) Thomas L. Pratt, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Dover, Del. Sir: You will act as a counsel for commandant of Fort Delaware, in Habeas Corpus Cases before Judge Hall, as to prisoners confined at that Fort. Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
