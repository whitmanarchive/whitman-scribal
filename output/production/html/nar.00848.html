March 20, 1869. Messrs. Dunbar, Waters, & Co. 29 Court st. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: Please find enclosed a check for $12— for subscription for the Boston Daily Advertiser for one year from March 18, 1869. Very Resp. M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk.
