October 18, 1869. Wm. C. Kneffner, Esq. Assessor, 12th District, Illinois, Belleville, Ill. Sir: I am directed to inform you that no Opinion has yet been given by the Attorney General on the question of the right of Internal Revenue officers to shares as informers, referred to in your letter of the 14th inst. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Int. Revenue officers as informers, &c.
