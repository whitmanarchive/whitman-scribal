October 19, 1869. Hon. C. C. Wilson, Chief Justice, Salt Lak​ City, Utah Terr. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that in accordance with your request of the 15th inst. you are hereby granted leave of absence for forty (40) days from November 1st, 1869. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Leave of absence.
