June 29, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court. U. S. Sir: I transmit herewith a transcript of the record in The United States plaintiffs in Error, vs. Alfred Huger & Charles T. Lowndes, defendants, writ of error to the Circuit Court of the United States of America for the District of South Carolina. Please file and docket it Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Act'g Attorney General.
trans. record.
