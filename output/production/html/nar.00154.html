Oct. 24, 1866. To L. V.B. Martin, Esq. U. S. Dist. Attorney Mobile, Ala. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to send you the enclosed copy of a letter from the late Attorney General to your predecessor, relative to the case of J. M. Fomeny. Mr. Worrall failed to make any reply to it, & it is quite possible that he also failed to notify you of it. These instructions have not been modified, & you are to be guided by them in the matter. Very respectfully, &c M. F. Pleasants, Act'g. Chief Clerk.
