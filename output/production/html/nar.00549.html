June 2, 1868. Hon. Perry E. Brocchus, Associate Justice, Supreme Court, Santa Fé, N. Mex. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney Gen'l ad interim to say that your application for leave of absence from your post of duty for sixty days from the 1st of July next, is hereby granted. [Leave extended thirty days by letter of Aug 28, signed J. H.A.] Very respectfully yours M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
