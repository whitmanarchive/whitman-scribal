March 22, 1871. Hon. T. W. Osborn, U.S. Senate. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 20th instant, I have the honor to inform you that there are no employés in this office accredited to the state of Florida. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
