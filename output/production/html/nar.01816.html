April 15, 1871. Hon. Henry Caldwell, U. S. District Judge, Little Rock, Arkansas. The President wishes to know if there is any official unfaithfulness in the District Attorney or Marshal of your District, or any cause for removing either of them. Please answer. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General. chge.
