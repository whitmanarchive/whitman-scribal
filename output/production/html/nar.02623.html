Nov. 28, 1871. Hon. E. A. Rollins, Baltimore, Md. Sir: I am directed by the Solicitor General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 27th instant, and to inform you that copies of papers to which you referred have been sent to the U.S. Attorney at Baltimore by to-day's mail. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
