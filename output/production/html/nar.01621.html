January 13, 1871. Hon. D. H. Chamberlain, Attorney General of South Carolina. Sir: I have received your letter of the 9th instant, and in reply inform you that if the statistics of crime, &c., are forwarded by the 15th instant, they will reach me in time for my Annual Report to Congress. Please accept my thanks for your courtesy and attention in this matter. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
