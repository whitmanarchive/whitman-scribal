December 15, 1870. George Andrews, Esq. Knoxville, Tennessee. Sir: You are hereby retained for the next four months, at a compensation at the rate of Fifteen hundred dollars a year as assistant to the U. S. Attorney for the Eastern District of Tennessee. You will take the oath prescribed by law, and transmit a copy to this office. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Ass't to U. S. Att'y Ea. Tenn.
