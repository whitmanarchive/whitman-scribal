Attorney General's Office, March 17, 1866. To Charles C. Fulton & Son, Baltimore, Md. Gents: Enclosed I send you Nine, (9) dollars, for subscription to the "Daily American" from Jan'y 28, 1865, to Jany 28, 1866. I am directed by the Atty Gen'l, to return your bill for '67, and to say that he cannot prepay for the papers rec'd at this office. Please acknowledge receipt. Very Respectfully Wm Stewart Chief Clerk
