January 19, 1870. Hon. Wm. Loughridge, House of Representatives. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to say that he has forwarded to the Secretary of the Treasury the opinion on the Bounty Law of March 3d, 1869, referred to in your letter of the 13th ultimo. Very respectfully, &c M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
