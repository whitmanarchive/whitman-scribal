April 8, 1871. W. W. Willoughby, Esq. Alexandria, Va. Sir: In compliance with your request, I return herewith the papers filed in this Department, in support of your application for the position of Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the District of Columbia. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk
