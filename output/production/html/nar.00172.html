Dec. 22, 1866. John D. Martin, Esq. Lancaster, Ohio. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 20th inst. enclosing accounts of R. M. Clarke, U. S. Attorney for the Dist. of Nevada, & to say that the papers have been referred to the First Comptroller of the Treasury. Respectfully, &c M. F. Pleasants Ch. Clerk.
