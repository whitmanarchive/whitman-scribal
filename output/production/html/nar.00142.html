Oct. 20, 1866. To Hon. Hugh McCulloch, Sec. of Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the U. S. in favor of S. M. McKean, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Dep't. for the sum of One Thousand nine hundred and eighty-eight dollars, and thirty eight cents, ($1,988:38) and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very respectfully Henry Stanbery, Att'y. Gen'l. Salary of the Attorney General for October, 1866, $630.11 "      "  "   Assistant Att'y Gen'l. "       "   282.63 "      "  Clerks & Messenger 1075:64 Total—$1,988:38
