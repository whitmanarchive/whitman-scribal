January 30, 1871. Alexander Garden, Esq. Anacostia, D. C. Sir: I enclose herewith a commission appointing you to be a member of the Levy Court of the County of Washington, District of Columbia. You will be pleased to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
