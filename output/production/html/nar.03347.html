Sept. 18, 1871. Hon. J. W. Douglass, Commissioner of Internal Revenue. Sir: In the absence of the Attorney General, I enclose herewith a copy of a telegram to him from his friend, J. W. H. Underwood, of Georgia, relative to the memorial of Catheren and Maguire. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
