February 11, 1869. Hon. Henry L. Warren, Ch. Justice Sup. Court Montana T. Sir: You are hereby allowed a leave of absence from your post of official duty for two months from February 1st, 1869, by direction of the Attorney General. It is taken for granted by this office that this leave of absence will be in accordance with the proper performance of the judicial duties of your position. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
