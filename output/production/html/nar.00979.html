August 6, 1869. Hon. J. D. Cox, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the accounts of certain Assistants to U. S. Attorneys, in place of others disallowed in part, and returned to this office to be made out anew—and to request their payment from the appropriate fund—they having been approved by me. Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
Acc'ts of Assistant U. S. Attorneys.
