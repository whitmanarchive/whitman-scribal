July 5, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to transmit herewith the transcript of the record in the case of Samuel A. Cooley & Henry G. Judd, plaintiffs in error, vs. Mary O'Connor, def't, in error, to be filed and docketed; and to request you to enter his appearance for the plaintiff in error. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
Sup. Court case.
