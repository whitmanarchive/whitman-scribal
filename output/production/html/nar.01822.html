April 15, 1871. Hon. B. F. Potts, Governor of Montana Territory Helena, M. T. Sir: I have received yours of the 5th instant. After the first of this month, instructions went from this office to the Marshal of your Territory to take possession of the Penitentiary of that Territory. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
