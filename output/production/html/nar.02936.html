March 4, 1871. Hon. George M. Robeson, Secretary of the Navy. Sir: I have the honor to enclose the letter of Thomas P. Saffold, Esq. of Georgia, accepting the appointment of Visitor to the Naval Academy at Annapolis. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman.
T. P. Saffold, Geo.
