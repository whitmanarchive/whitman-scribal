March 10, 1868. Hon. W. R. Gorsline, Associate Justice, Colorado Terr. Sheboygan, Wis. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that the leave of absence previously granted to you is hereby extended to the 15th of April, 1868. Very Respectfully yours M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
