Feb. 25, 1871. T. H. Talbot, Esq. Assistant Attorney General. Sir: Mr. Shroder is authorized to appear for the United States in the Court of Claims in the seven cases mentioned in your letter of the 23d instant. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Mr. Shroder
