October 21, 1869. Walter F. Austin, Esq. No. 96 Diamond street, Pittsburgh, Pa. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say, in reply to your letter of the 18th inst. that he will give his attention to any written statement you may desire to make, relating to the question of bounties, under the Act of March 3, 1869. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
