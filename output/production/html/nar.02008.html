June 6, 1871. John A. Lynch, Esq. Washington City. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 2d instant, and to request you to call upon me at this Department, at whatever time may be convenient for yourself. Very respectfully, &c. A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
