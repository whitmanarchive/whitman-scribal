March 7, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith for your immediate consideration a copy of a letter transmitted to me by the District Attorney of the Northern District of New York, relative to rumored projects for the invasion of Canada. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
Invasion of Canada.
