February 17, 1870. Hon. C. Delano, Commissioner Internal Revenue. Sir: The letter of which the enclosed is a copy was addressed to the U. S. Dist. Attorney for New Jersey, in reply to an unofficial communication from him in relation to the proposed compromise of the Internal Revenue suit against Thorne and his sureties. Very respectfully yours, W. A. Field, Assistant Attorney General. (letter enc.)
