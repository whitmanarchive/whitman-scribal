February 5, 1869. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: I am directed to request you to enter the appearance of the Attorney General for the plaintiff in error, in the case of J. W. Gates, Pl'ff. in error, vs. D. M. Osborne & al., and to send to this office the record as soon as it is printed. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
