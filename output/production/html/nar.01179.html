January 28, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: Will you please put the accompanying paper on file? By doing so you will oblige, Yours truly, W. A. Field, Ass't Attorney General.
To Cl'k U. S. Sup. Ct.
