Oct. 10, 1871. Hon. John H. Howe, Chief Justice, Cheyenne, Wyoming Terr. Sir: In compliance with your request, leave of absence is hereby granted until the 1st proximo. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
