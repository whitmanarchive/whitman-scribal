July 28, 1868. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. No. 535 Chestnut st. Philadelphia. Gentlemen: Please find enclosed, and acknowledge the receipt of a check for $107:50, the amount of your receipted bills to July 24, 1868, against this office. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
