May 12, 1869. P. V. R. Van Wyck, Esq. 95 Liberty st. New York. Sir. The Attorney General requests that you will send to this office a copy of the Internal Revenue Record, and Customs Journal, from its commencement. You will please send duplicate bills receipted, when a check will be returned. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk
