April 10, 1871. Hon. Philip B. Swing, Cincinnati, Ohio. Sir: Yours of the 6th instant, enclosing a copy of the official oath which you have taken, has been received. Let me remind you that you should also take the oath required by the Act of July 2, 1862, (12 U. S. Stat, 502.) Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
