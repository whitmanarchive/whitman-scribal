August 14, 1868. John W. Daniel, Esq. Box 96, Lynchburg, Va. Sir: In reply to your inquiry of the 11th instant, you are informed that the "Opinions of the Attorneys General" are not published as Public Documents, for gratuitous circulation, but by contract with W. H. & O. H. Morrison, booksellers of this city. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
