November 15, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have received your letter of the 12th inst., in relation to the suits against Fraser, Trenholm & Co., enclosing a copy of a letter received by you from Mr. Silliman, of counsel for the trustees of Fraser, Trenholm & Co. I will communicate with Mr. Cushing, and endeavor to have the forms of the judgments prepared by the time that Mr. Silliman arrives in Washington. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Fraser, Trenholm & Co's case.
