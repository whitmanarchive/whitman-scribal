December 30, 1867. Hon. William H. Seward, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith for your information, by direction of the Attorney General, a copy of a letter just received from Edward C. Carrington, Esq. United States Attorney for the District of Columbia. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General. [copy enc.]
