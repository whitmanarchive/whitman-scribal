July 7, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: Your letter of the 6th inst. submits for my consideration the Internal Revenue case of A. W. Bayer & Co. of Kansas. The terms of the compromise proposed are that Bayer & Co. pay
as tax $922.75
Assessed penalty — 804.25
Specific penalty 250.00
In all 1977.00, and costs.
I recommend that these terms be accepted. I have the honor to be, &c, &c. E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
