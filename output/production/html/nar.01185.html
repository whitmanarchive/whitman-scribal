February 4, 1870. Hon. W. M. Stewart: Dear Sir: Any facts which you or Judge Field desire to know in relation to "one Murphy,"'s case are much at your service. But it is hardly practicable to undertake to consider the matter by correspondence. Very truly yours, E. R. Hoar. (letter enc.)
Case of N. S. Murphy, Cal.
