Dec. 18, 1871. R. McPhail Smith, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Nashville, Tenn. Sir: I enclose an appointment of Harry Harrison, Esq. as Assistant to the District Attorney for the Middle District of Tennessee, to take effect on the 10th proximo. You will deliver the same to Mr. Harrison. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
H. Harrison Ass't.
