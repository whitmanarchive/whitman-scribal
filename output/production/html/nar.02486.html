Sept. 25, 1871. Jacob F. Wright, Esq. Indianapolis, Ind. Sir: I have received your letter of the 21st instant, and, in reply, inform you that this Department has no copies of the opinion of the Attorney General to which you refer, for distribution. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
