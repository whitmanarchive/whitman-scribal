Feb. 22, 1871. Hon. H. B. Anthony, U.S. Senate. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to enclose herewith a copy of the recommendation of the members of the bar of Rhode Island for the appointment of James H. Coggeshall as Marshal of the United States for that District. Very respectfully, &c. A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
a copy of Mr. Coggeshall's recommendations
