December 24, 1869. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. Philadelphia, Penn. Gentlemen: I herewith transmit a list of State Reports, on the margin of which you will find noted the prices at which you have heretofore stated these books could be furnished by you. If they can now be supplied by you at rates not exceeding those mentioned on said list, you are requested to forward them to this office, with your account (in duplicate) receipted. Very resp. &c. &c W. A. Field, Acting Attorney Gen'l.
