Feb. 14, 1871. Thos. G. Newman, Esq. Fox Lake, Lodge Co. Wisconsin. Sir: I have received your letter of the 3d instant, asking for information as to how to proceed to obtain a copy of your discharge papers, the original having been lost; and inform you that it has been referred to the Secretary of War—the subject being one with which I have no connection, and he being the proper officer to whom your application should have been made. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
