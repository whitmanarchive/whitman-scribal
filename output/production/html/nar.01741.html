Feb. 23, 1871. Amos Pillsbury, Esq. Superintendent Albany Penitentiary, Albany, New York. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 16th inst. and to thank you for your kindness in sending him the copies of the Report, and the rules and regulations of the Albany Penitentiary. Very respectfully, &c A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
Report &c acknowledged.
