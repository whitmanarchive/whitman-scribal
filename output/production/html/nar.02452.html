Sept. 8, 1871. Charles Bradshaw, Care E. V. C. Blake, Clerk U. S. Dist. Court, Montgomery, Ala. If you report on the 15th inst. you will be appointed to a Twelve hundred dollar clerkship in this Department. Answer if you accept. Letter to you by this day's mail. A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
