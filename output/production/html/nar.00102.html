Attorney General's Office, April 12, 1866. To Isaac Hartman, Esq. 24 Stevinson House San Francisco, Cal. Sir: I am instructed by Mr. Ashton to say that the cases mentioned in your telegram of the 9th inst. have not been examined yet, but will be very soon. I am sir, very respectfully Wm. Stewart Chief Clerk.
