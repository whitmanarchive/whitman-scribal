December 28, 1869. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson, & Co. Philadelphia. Gentlemen: Your letter of the 27th inst. has been received. You may furnish all the later Reports mentioned therein, except vol 36 How. Prac. Rep.; and in addition thereto you are requested to send us Abbot's Prac. Rep. (N. series) vols 5 & 6. Very resp. &c. M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
