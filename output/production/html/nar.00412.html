January 24, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk United States Supreme Court Sir: The Attorney General requests that you will send to this Office a copy of the Record in the case of Geo. A. Gardner vs. Hiram Barney, No. 106 Very respectfully, yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
