Dec. 16, 1871. S. R. Hollyday, Esq. Secretary of State, Annapolis, Md. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 13th instant, transmitting to this Department statistics of crime asked for in his circular letter to the Governor, of the 16th of October last; and to thank you for your courtesy and attention to his request. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
