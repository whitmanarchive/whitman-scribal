Sept. 13, 1871. Thomas H. Talbot, Esq, Assistant Attorney General. Sir: After consideration of the case of Rebecca A. G. Minor, Executrix of William J. Minor, deceased, which is the subject of your letter of the 22d ultimo, I think that it is not worth while to take or prosecute an appeal therein. Very respectfully, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l, & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
