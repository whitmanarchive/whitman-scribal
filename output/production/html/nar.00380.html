December 30, 1867. Hon. L. P. Williston Associate Justice, Supreme Court, Montana Terr. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence for three months from February 1st, 1868, in accordance with your request of the 2d instant. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
