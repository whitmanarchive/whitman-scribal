March 11, 1871. Hon. R. Strickland, St. Johns, Michigan. Dear sir: I find on my table the copy of your recommendations from Mr. Blaine and Mr. Bingham. It occurs to me that you may like to retain it, and therefore I forward it to you. Very respectfully yours, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
