March 19, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the papers in the case of Amos Reed, locator of certain lands in Nebraska, endorsed with the concurrence of the Attorney General, ad interim, for the confirmation of the entries. Respectfully, your obedient serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
