June 19, 1869. Hon. James K. Kennedy, Asso. Justice, Port Townsend, Washington T. Sir: I have rec'd your letter of the 2d inst. enclosing a copy of your official oath, and making an inquiry as to your proper District. In reply I have to say that you were nominated and confirmed in the place of James E. Wyche. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
