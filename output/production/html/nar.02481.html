Sept. 21, 1871 Walter Evans, Esq. Hopkinsville Ky. Can you accept immediate employment to investigate professionally alleged frauds in Tennessee, for thirty days, at ten dollars a day, and your expenses? If so come immediately here. Answer. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General and Acting Attorney General. ch'ge
