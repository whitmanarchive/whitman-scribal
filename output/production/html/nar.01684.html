Feb. 4, 1871. Hon. John L. Murphy, Asso. Justice Supreme Court, Montana Terr. Sir: I enclose herewith a Commission appointing you to the Office of Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of Montana Territory. You will be pleased to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
