June 7, 1869. Hon. John A. Rawlins, Secretary of War. Sir: Understanding that the question submitted by your predecessor to this Department, by letter of September 25, 1868, has become of no farther practical importance, I return the papers connected therewith,— and am, Very Respectfully, &c. &c. E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
