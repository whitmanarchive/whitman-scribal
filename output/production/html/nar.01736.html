Feb. 15, 1871. Hon. R. W. Taylor, 1st Comptroller of the Treasury. Sir: I enclose herewith a copy of my Opinion of the 21st ultimo, as to the appellate jurisdiction of the Department of Justice in the matter of the accounts of Marshals, Attorneys, Clerks, and Commissioners of the Courts of the United States. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
