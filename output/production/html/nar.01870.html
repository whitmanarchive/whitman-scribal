May 19, 1871. Messrs. Hendricks, Howe, & Hendricks; Indianapolis, Ind. Gentlemen: Your letter of the 9th instant, enclosing a transcript of the record in the case of Wm. F. Reynolds, Trustee, vs. John S. Williams, has been received, and the transcript placed on file in the clerk's office of the Supreme Court of the United States. I am, very respectfully, your ob't servant. Clement Hugh Hill, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
