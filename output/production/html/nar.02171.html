Nov. 17, 1871. Geo. H. Sharp​ Sharpe​ , U. S. Marshal, New York. Solicitor General Bristow goes to New York to-night, and will advise with you. He will be at the Fifth Avenue Hotel at breakfast, when you can see him. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
