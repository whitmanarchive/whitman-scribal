March 29, 1871. George B. Corkhill, Esq. Clerk of Circuit Court for Dist. of Iowa, Des Moines, Iowa. In United States, vs. certain caddies of Tobacco, J. N. Henderson & Co. claimants, no service of writ of error, and no citation appears in record. Is there any in your files? Please answer at once. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General.
