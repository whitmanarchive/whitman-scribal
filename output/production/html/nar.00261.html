October 30, 1867. Hon. Jos. H. Barrett, Commissioner of Pensions. Sir: In compliance with your request in letter of October 24, I send you, as follows, a list of the gentlemen who, at this date, occupy the position of U. S. Dist. Attorneys in the different States and Territories. Very respectfully, yours, Henry Stanbery, Attorney General. [List appended.]
