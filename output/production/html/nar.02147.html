Nov. 11, 1871. M. J. Patrick, Esq. U.S. Marshal, Salt Lake City, Utah. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 3d instant, and to inform you that leave of absence for ten days will be granted to you as requested. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
