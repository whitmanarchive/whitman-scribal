March 9, 1871. Hon. J. B. Chaffee, House of Representatives. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 7th instant, I have to inform you that no such letter as you refer to has been received by this Department from L. C. Rockwell, the U.S. Attorney for Colorado. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
