October 14, 1869. Geo. F. Peall, Esq. 933 Randolph street Philadelphia. Sir: I am directed to say, in reply to your letter of the 13th inst. that this office has no information in regard to the commandant of Camp Parole, Annapolis, Md. during the late war. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
