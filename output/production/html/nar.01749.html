Feb. 24, 1871. Hon. John M. McKinney, District Judge U. S. So. Dist, Florida Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a commission appointing you to be District Judge of the United States for the Southern District of Florida. You will be pleased to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
