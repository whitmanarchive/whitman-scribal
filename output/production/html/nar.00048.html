Attorney General's Office. August 22, 1865. John L. Williamson Esq. Memphis, Tenn. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit herewith a Commission, appointing you to be Attorney of the U. S. for the W. Dist. of Tennessee; and to request that you send to this office, along with a copy of your official oath, a statement of the place of your nativity, for the Biennial Register. I am sir, very Resp. your obd't sv't, Wm. Stewart, Chief Clerk. [Com. enc. with above.]
