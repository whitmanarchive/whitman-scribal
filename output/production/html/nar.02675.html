Dec. 18, 1871. Publishers Cincinnati Commercial, Sirs: Please send a copy of your daily paper to Hon. Wm. McMichael, Ass't. Atty. Gen'l. at Court of Claims, Washington, D. C.; and send your bill for one year's subscription to me for payment. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
Cincinnati Commercial
