November 10, 1868. Hon. C. C. Wilson, Chief Justice, Salt Lake City, Utah. Sir; I am directed by the Attorney General to say that your application for leave of absence from your official post, from December 15, 1868, is hereby granted, to extend to a period of sixty days. Very respectfully, W. F. Peddrick, Acting Ch. Clerk.
