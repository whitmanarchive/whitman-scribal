May 10, 1869. James W. Locko, Esq. Clerk of the U. S. Dist. Court, Key West, Fla. Sir: I send enclosed mandates of the Supreme Court in the cases of The schooner "John Williams" vs. The U. S. " steamer "Union" & cargo vs. U. S. " schooner "Jane" & cargo vs. U. S. " schooner "Theresa" & cargo vs. U. S. " United States, vs. Sch'r "Diana" — — and ask that you will file them in the Court. Very Respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
