June 24, 1868. John Carroll Brent, Esq. Counsellor at Law, City Hall, Washington, D. C. Sir: Responding to your oral inquiry of the 23d instant, I herewith enclose a copy of our instructions of this date to the United States Attorney at Montgomery, Alabama. Respectfully, your ob't serv't. John M. Binckley. Assistant Attorney General [copy enc.]
