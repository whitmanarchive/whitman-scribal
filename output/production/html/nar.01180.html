January 29, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 27th inst. I have the honor to transmit herewith a tabular statement giving the names of all officers now in the Territories, whose commissions are issued by your Department, upon requisition from this office. I have not included officers in the District of Columbia, as they are not called for by the Resolution of the Senate. Very respectfully, Your obedient servant, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
List of certain Territorial officers.
