Nov. 20, 1871. A. Stirling, Jr. Esq. U. S. Attorney, Baltimore, Md. Sir: Your letter of the 17th inst. enclosing the transcript of the record in the case of The United States vs. The Baltimore and Ohio RR. Company from the Circuit Court for the District of Maryland, has been received. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
