Nov. 1, 1871. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith estimates of the Appropriations required for the service of this Department for the fiscal year ending June 30, 1873. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Appropriations, 1872-3.
