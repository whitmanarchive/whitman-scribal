November 8, 1867. T. W. Brown, Esq. Memphis, Tenn. Sir: Your letter of the 4th inst. is received, and I am directed by the Attorney General to say that in his opinion the case elaborated in your letter is not now in such a position as to authorize him to interfere. Very respectfully, your obdt servant F. U. Stitt, Acting Chief Clerk.
