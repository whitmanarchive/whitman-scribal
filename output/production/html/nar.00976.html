August 4, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: The enclosed letter from the Assistant U. S. Attorney at New Orleans dated July 31st, ult., with a copy of a letter sent by him to the Secretary of the Interior, are respectfully referred to the Secretary of the Treasury for such consideration as he may think fit. Very Respectfully, your obedient serv't, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General
