Attorney General's Office, Washington, July 17, 1865. Lawrence Weldon, Esq. U. S. Attorney, S. Dist. Illinois, Springfield, Ill. Sir: I am directed by the Att'y General to transmit herewith a Commission appointing you Attorney of the United States for the Southern District of Illinois; and to request that you will send to this Office, along with a copy of your official oath, a statement of the place of your birth, for the Biennial register. I am Sir very respectfully, Your obedient serv't Wm Stewart, Chief Clerk.
