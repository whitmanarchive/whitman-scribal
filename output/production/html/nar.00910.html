May 8, 1869. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court U. S. Sir: Will you please send to this office a certified copy of the opinion of the Court in the Texas case, so‑called, in which it was decided that Texas was so far a State of the United States as to be able to maintain a suit in the Courts of the United States. Very Resp. yours, W. A. Field, Ass't Attorney Gen'l.
Copy of Sup. Court opinion requested.
