October 8, 1866. To Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit herewith the acc't., approved by him, & amounting to Five hundred dollars, ($500.00) of Lewis S. Dabney, for services as assistant to Richard H. Dana, jr. U. S. Attorney for the District of Massachusetts, from July 1st 1866, to October 1st 1866; & to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Act'g. Ch'f. Clerk.
