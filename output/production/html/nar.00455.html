March 7, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by the Attorney General, and amounting to One Hundred Dollars, of Messrs. Sale & Dowd, for legal services in assisting James E. Stewart, Esq. United States Attorney for the Northern District of Mississippi in the prosecution and conviction of certain mail robbers; and to request its payment from the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, Sir, Your obedient servant, John M. Binckley Assistant Att'y Gen'l.
