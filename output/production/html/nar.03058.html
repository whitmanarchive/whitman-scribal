May 8?9, 1871. General A. Pleasanton, Commissioner of Internal Revenue. Sir: The question raised by Mr. Given, Deputy Commissioner of Internal Revenue, in his communication to you of April 6, and by you referred to me, has been submitted to Mr. Hill, Assistant Attorney General, whose opinion, approved, is herewith transmitted. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
