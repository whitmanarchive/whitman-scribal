May 6, 1869. Hon. A. M. Clapp, Congressional Printer. Sir: I have to request that you will furnish for the use of this Office Two hundred and fifty, (250,) copies of the pamphlet edition of the Laws of the last two sessions of Congress. Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Act'g Attorney Gen'l.
