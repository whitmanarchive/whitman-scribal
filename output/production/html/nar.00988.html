August 13, 1869. To the Secretary of State. Sir: At the request of the Marshal of the United States for the Southern Dist. of New York, I have the honor to transmit herewith a bill, received this morning, for the services and, expenses of certain detective police officers, in the investigation of the Cuban fili bustering​ filibustering​ Expeditions. Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
Bill of detectives.
