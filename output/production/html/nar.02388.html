Aug 7, 1871. Messrs. Hall and Lowe, Steubenville, Ohio. Gentlemen: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 3d inst, and to inform you that this Department has no copies of the opinions of the Attorney General for distribution. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk​ .​
