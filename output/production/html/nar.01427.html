July 21, 1870. Hon. J. D. Cox, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: By direction of the President I send you the enclosed papers. His final action in the matter appears in his endorsement of July 20, 1870. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
transmitting papers, &c.
