June 6, 1870. Hon. C. C. Wilson, Chief Justice, Utah Terr. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are allowed a leave of absence from your Judicial District from May 28, 1870 to June 20, 1870. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
