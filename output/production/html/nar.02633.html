Dec. 1, 1871. V. S. Luske, Esq. Raleigh, N. C. Sir: I have received your letter of the 27th ultimo, enclosing your account for two months' services, rendered from August 1 to September 30, 1870, and for three hundred and seventy (370) miles travelled by you during that time—and inform you that the account has been approved and referred to the First Auditor of the Treasury for settlement. A draft for the amount thereof will be sent to you in the regular course of business. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
acc't approved &c.
