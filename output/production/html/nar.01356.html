June 4, 1870. Hon. Wm. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the papers transmitted to me with your letter of the 7th of April last, requesting my opinion on the Richmond Mayoralty case, that case being settled, an​ and​ no opinion being now desired in reference to it. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar Attorney General
