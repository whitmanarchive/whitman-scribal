March 3, 1868 Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: In compliance with the request contained in the letter of John C. Cox, Chief Clerk of your Department, of the 2d instant, I have the honor to transmit herewith a complete List of the Judges of the District Courts and Territories of the United States. I am, Sir, Very Respectfully, Your obedient servant, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
