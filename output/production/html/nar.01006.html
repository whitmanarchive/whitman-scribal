September 29, 1869. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chicago, Illinois. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 27th inst., preferring charges against the official conduct of John Logan, U. S. Marshal of the Southern District of Illinois, and to say that they will receive the immediate and careful attention of this Department. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
Charges ag't. J. Logan, U. S. Marshal
