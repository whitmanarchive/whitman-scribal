October 31, 1870. Hon. Roger S. Greene, Asso. Justice, Olympia, Washington T. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 18th inst., in relation to your absence from the Territory "during the coming winter," and to ask when you desire your leave of absence to begin, and whether its period will cover any session of your court. Very respectfully, &c M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
concerning leave of absence.
