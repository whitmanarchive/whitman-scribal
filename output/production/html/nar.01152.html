January 10, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith an account forwarded to this office by the U. S. Attorney for the District of Vermont, for services rendered in relation to the suspected invasion of Canada by the Fenians. Very respectfully, Your obd't serv't, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
Bill of U. S. Attorney, Vt.
