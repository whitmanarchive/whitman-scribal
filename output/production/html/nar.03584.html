Dec. 22, 1871. Hon. C. Delano, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the adjudication of the Commissioner of the General Land Office, and other papers relating to the suspended land entry of Daniel Nolen in the Stocton, California, District, and to state that I concur in the adjudication of the Commissioner. I am sir, very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
