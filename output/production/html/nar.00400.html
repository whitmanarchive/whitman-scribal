January 16, 1868. Associate Justice Supreme Court, Bonhomme, Dakota Terr. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that your application, dated January 1, 1868, for leave of absence for thirty days, is hereby granted. Very Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
