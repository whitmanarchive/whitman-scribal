October 22, 1870. S. Hepburn, Jr. Esq. Carlisle, Penn. Sir: Enclosed I return your retainer as special assistant to the U. S. Attorney for the Eastern Dist. of Pennsylvania, to take charge of the case of Lieut. Wm. T. Cain, 3d U. S. Cavalry, having retained a copy of your official oath. Very Resp'ly, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk. (enc.)
retainer &c.
