December 29, 1869. Hon. E. R. Hoar, U. S. Attorney General, Concord, Mass. District Attorney Noble has resigned. President asks if it would not be well to request him to reconsider it.—Shall it be done? W. A. Field, Act'g Attorney General.
Dis't. Attorney Noble's resignation
