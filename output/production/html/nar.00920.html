May 12, 1869. Messrs. Little, Brown, & Co. 110 Washington st. Boston. Gentlemen: The Attorney General requests that you will furnish this office with the following books: Woolsey's International Law. Case of the Meteor. Parsons on Contracts, (last ed'n.) Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk.
