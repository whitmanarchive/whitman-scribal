March 25, 1871. James A. Ruthven, Esq. No. 33 Nassau st, New York. Sir: I have received yours of the 20th instant, and in reply inform you that the Supreme Court has determined to hear argument again on the legal tender question, and that the 5th of April is the assigned day. Very resp'lly. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
