November 11, 1867. W. G. Dickson, Esq. United States Marshal, Savannah, Geo. Sir: This office is in receipt of your letter of the 5th inst.; and in reply I have to say that the error in the letter of instructions from this Office, of date of 31st ultimo, was purely and altogether a clerical one. It was immediately corrected, and duplicate originals were furnished to the Hon. Caleb Cushing, which, I presume, you have now received. Very respectfully, &c. F. U. Stitt, Acting Chief Clerk.
