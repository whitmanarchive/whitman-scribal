October 20, 1866. To Hon. Hugh McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States, in favor of S. M. McKean, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Two thousand and seventy-eight dollars and twenty seven cents, ($2,078:27,) and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very respectfully, &c. Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
