February 6, 1869. Hon. J. P. Kidder, Asso. Just. Sup. Court Vermillion, Dakota T. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are hereby allowed a leave of absence for thirty days from the 15th inst. to transact important personal business without the Ter. Respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk
