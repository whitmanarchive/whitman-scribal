May 24, 1871. Publishers of the Nation. Gentlemen: Enclosed please find five dollars for one year's subscription to the "Nation" for this Department. Be pleased to receipt, and return enclosed bill to me. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
