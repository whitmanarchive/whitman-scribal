October 30, 1869. Gen'l T. W. Egan, Ebbitt House, Washington, D. C. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say in reply to your note, of this date that recommendations for office transmitted to this Department are considered to belong to its files, and it is against the rules of the office to return them. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
declining return of papers.
