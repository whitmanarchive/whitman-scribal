Attorney General's Office, April 9, 1866. To R. M. Corwine, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Cincinnati, Ohio. Sir: Your letter of the 6th of April is at hand. I return the account of Judge W. T. Gholson with a request that it be forwarded in duplicate. On its receipt in duplicate, I will order its payment. The course indicated by you in regard to costs is approved. I am, sir, most respectfully, James Speed Attorney General.
