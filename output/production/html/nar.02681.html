Dec. 22, 1871. Hon. Edward G. Bradford, Wilmington, Del. Sir: I enclose herewith a Commission appointing you to the office of District Judge of the U.S. for the District of Delaware; and if the same be accepted I will thank you to transmit to this Department a copy of your official oath, and to inform me of the place of your nativity. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
