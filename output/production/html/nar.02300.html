Dec. 28, 1871. D. J. Baldwin, Esq. U. S. Attorney. Galveston, Texas. Sir: Your satisfactory Report of the 21st in relation to the case against Jesse Stancel has been received. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
