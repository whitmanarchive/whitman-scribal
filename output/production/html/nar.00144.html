October 22, 1866. To Hon. W. F. Lockwood, Associate Just. Sup. Ct. Dakotah, Neb. T. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to say that you are hereby granted, according to request, leave of absence for three months, commencing Nov. 1st, 1866. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Act'g Chief Clerk.
