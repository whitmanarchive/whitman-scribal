September 21, 1869. Hon. J. J. Martin Sixth Auditor of the Treasury. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to inform you that the mandate of the Supreme Court in the case of Ware & Baird, vs. U. S., has this day been received from the Clerk, and forwarded to the U. S. Dist. Attorney at Philadelphia, Aubrey H. Smith, Esq. Very Respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Sup. Ct. Mand. case of Ware & Baird, vs. U. S.
