Feb. 27, 1869. Hon. H. McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request you to issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the U. S. in favor of Messrs. Sumner & Culter, of Washington, D. C. for the sum of Two Thousand dollars, ($2000:) and charge the same to the appropriation for legal assistance, & other necessary expenditures in the disposal of private land claims in California. Very resp. yours, Wm M. Evarts, Attorney General.
