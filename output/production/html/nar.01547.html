December 15, 1870. Hon Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I am directed by the President to request you to issue a commission appointing Clement Hugh Hill to the office of Assistant Attorney General, agreeably to the enclosed resolution of the Senate. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
commission to Clement Hugh Hill Ass't. Atty. Gen'l.
