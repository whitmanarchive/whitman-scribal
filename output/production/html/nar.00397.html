January 11, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: In accordance with the request contained in your letter of the 10th inst. I have the honor to enclose herewith a list of Justices of the Peace in office September 30th, 1867, for the Official Register. Very respectfully, Your obedient servant, Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
