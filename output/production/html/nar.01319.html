April 14, 1870. Hon. Hannibal Hamlin, Chairman Com. on Dist. of Columbia, U. S. Senate. Sir: In conformity with your request, I enclose herewith the papers relating to the case of David R. Smith, nominated for a Justice of the Peace. Will you please to return them when you have done with them? Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
