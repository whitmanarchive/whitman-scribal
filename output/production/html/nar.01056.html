October 29, 1869. J. Smith Homans, Esq. 41 Pine street, New York. Sir: Your letter of the 25th inst. to the Secretary of State, requesting a list of U. S. Circuit Judges, Attorneys, Marshals, &c. has been referred to this Office. The information desired is all contained in the "Official Register of the United States for 1869," now in the hands of the printer. It will be published probably in the course of a few weeks. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
List of Judges, &c. wanted.
