Dec. 6, 1871, D. T. Corbin, Esq. U.S. Attorney, Columbia, S. C. Sir: Your two letters of the 3d inst. have been received. I am glad to have an account of the Enforcement trials. Please continue to advise me as you find time. Your account for services I will consider and act upon very soon. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
