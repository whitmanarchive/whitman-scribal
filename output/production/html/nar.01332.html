April 19, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court United States. Sir: Have you sent us an opinion in the case of the United States ex relatione Robert Murray, vs. The State of New York? Also in the case of Bennett vs. Hunter? If not will you be kind enough to do so at your earliest convenience? Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Assistant Attorney General.
Sup. Court Doc's. requested.
