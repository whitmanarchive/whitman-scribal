December 24, 1870. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of Twenty copies of the Annual Report of your Department on the state of the finances for 1870, for which be pleased to accept my thanks. Very respectfully, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General, and Acting Attorney General.
