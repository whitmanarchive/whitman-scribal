December 4, 1868. Hon. T. Lyle Dickey, Assistant Attorney General. Sir: I transmit herewith a copy of a letter from the Secretary of War, of the 2d instant, requesting to be informed, in future, of any judgments of the Court of Claims affecting the War Department—and also whether any such judgments have been rendered, since the publication of the third volume of Nott and Huntington's Reports. You will please see to it that the request of the Secretary be complied with. Very respectfully yours, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General.
Judgments ag't War Dep't.
