January 7, 1868. Benj. L. Price, Esq. 20 John street, New York. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to inform you, in reply to your letter of the 7th ultimo, that, on his motion, the case of the United States, vs. the Schooner Isabel & Cargo, has been docketed and dismissed. Very respectfully, &c Henry Stanbery Attorney General.
