January 20, 1871. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I am directed by the President to request you to issue Commissions appointing Stephen M. Golden and Alexander Garden to be members of the Levy Court of the County of Washington, District of Columbia, agreeably to the enclosed resolution of the Senate. I am, Sir, Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
