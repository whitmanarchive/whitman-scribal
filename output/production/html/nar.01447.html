October 31, 1870. W. R. Thrall, Esq. Columbus, Ohio. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 28th inst., and to say in reply that you shall be duly notified when it is desired that you shall qualify as U. S. Marshal. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
