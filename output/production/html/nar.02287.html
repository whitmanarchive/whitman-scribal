Dec. 26, 1871. Geo. C. Bates, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Salt Lake City, Utah. Sir: I am requested by Judge Williams to acknowledge the receipt of your letter to him bearing date the 16th instant, and to say that the subject therein referred to will receive his early attention. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk
