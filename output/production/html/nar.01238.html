March 3, 1870. Hon. James Harlan, Senator. Sir: Mr. Wilkins requests me to send you the enclosed papers from the files of my office—informing me that you wish to see them. I therefore send them, with the request that you will return them to this office. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar.
