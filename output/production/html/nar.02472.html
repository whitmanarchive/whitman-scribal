Sept. 14, 1871. N. W. Shafer, Esq. Attorney at Law. Pittsburgh, Pa. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 12th instant, I have to inform you that the Attorney General left for his home in Georgia on Wednesday evening last, to be absent several weeks. Very respectfully, Your ob't servant, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk
Atty Gen'l absent.
