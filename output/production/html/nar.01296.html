April 2, 1870. Isaac Caldwell, Esq. Louisville, Ky. Case of Blyew and Kennard cannot probably be postponed beyond the 20th instant, cannot you or some other counsel appear then, for State of Kentucky? Answer by telegraph, if you can come, and wish it postponed till the 20th inst. E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
