October 23, 1867. John E. Rosette, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Springfield, Ill. Sir: I have received yours of the 10th of October, 1867, and have now to instruct you to dismiss the libel in the case of The U. S. vs. 1756 Shares of the Great Western R. R. belonging to Leroy M. Wiley, upon the payment of the costs. Very respectfully yours, Henry Stanbery.
