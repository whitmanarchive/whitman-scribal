February 1, 1871. W. T. Samuels, Assistant Secretary of State. Frankfort, Ky. I want the law as it stood prior to 1st of October, 1868. Please send by Express. I will return. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General chg'e.
Tel.
send certain law.
