December 16, 1870. Wm. Craigh, Esq. Memphis, Tenn. Sir: You are hereby appointed an assistant to the U. S. Attorney for the Western District of Tennessee during the present term of the U. S. Court, with a compensation at the rate of Five hundred dollars per month. You will please transmit to this Department, with the acceptance of this appointment, a copy of your official oath. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General and Acting Attorney General.
ass't U. S. Attorney W. Tenn.
