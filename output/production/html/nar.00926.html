May 14, 1869. Edward Dexter, Esq. Clerk U. S. District Court, Boston, Mass. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, I transmit herewith the mandate of the U. S. Supreme Court, in the case of The U. S. vs. the stm'r Georgia, and ask that it be filed in the Court. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
