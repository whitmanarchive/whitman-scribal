May 19, 1870. Hon. A. A. Sargent, House of Representatives Sir: In reply to your inquiry of the 18th inst. the Attorney General directs me to say that the Treasury warrant in payment of the claim of Sumner & Cutter was for two thousand dollars, ($2000.) Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Inquiry ans.
