August 21, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to One thousand dollars, of H. H. Wells, Esq. for services as special counsel for the United States in the case of the United States vs. Jefferson Davis, and request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Respectfully, your obd't serv't, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General.
