December 24, 1869. Messrs, Little, Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: Enclosed herewith is a list of certain State Reports, with the prices noted thereon at which you proposed some time since to furnish these books to this office. If you can now supply the books mentioned on this list at rates not exceeding the prices there intimated, we shall be pleased to have you forward them with your account receipted, (in duplicate.) Very respectfully, Your obd't serv't, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney Gen'l.
