Nov. 22, 1871. G. H. Sharpe, Esq. U. S. Marshal, New York. Sir: You and your deputies will render the District Attorney any assistance in your power in discovering testimony for the Government in the cases in your District against William O. C. Ryan, and Thomas Jordan, for violation of the Neutrality Laws of the United States, and in other cases of similar character. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Ryan & Jordan's case
392,
N. Y.
