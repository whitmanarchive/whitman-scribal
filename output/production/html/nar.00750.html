January 23, 1869. Hon. G. M. Chilcott, Delegate from Colorado Territory. House of Representatives, Washington. Sir: I have the honor to enclose an answer to the application of Chief Justice Hallett for leave of absence from the Territory, which I beg you will transmit to him. I regret that other occupations have prevented my giving earlier attention to the application. Respectfully, &c, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General.
