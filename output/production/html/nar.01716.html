Feb. 10, 1871. Hon. B. R. Bullock, Governor of Georgia, Atlanta, Geo. Sir: I thank you for the statistics of crime under the laws of Georgia, just received from your Secretary. I regret that they were received too late for my Annual Report, which the law requires to be made in January. But I shall preserve them for use in a future Report. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
