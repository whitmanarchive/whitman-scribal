Feb. 19, 1869. Hon. H. McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States, in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. disbursing clerk, Treasury Dept., for the sum of $4662:73—and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very respectfully yours. Attorney General
