August 31, 1867. Delos Lake, Esq. U. S. Attorney, San Francisco, Cal. Sir: As requested in your letter of the 29th ult. I transmit you herewith the mandate in the case of Antonio Rocha et al Vs. the United States. Very Respectfully, yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
