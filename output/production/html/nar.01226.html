February 25, 1870. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Fifteen hundred dollars, ($1500:) and charge the same to the appropriation for "legal assistance and other necessary expenditures in the disposal of private land claims in California. ​ "​ Very respectfully, your obedient servant, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
Requisition for $1500
