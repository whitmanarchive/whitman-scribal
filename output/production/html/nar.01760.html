March 4, 1871. Mr. Job B. Overbury, Granville, Co. N. C. Sir: Your letter of the 24th ult. has been received. I do not understand whether the cases to which you refer are those between private parties only, or those to which the Government is a party. If the latter, you should apply to the U. S. District Attorney. If the former, I have no right to give official advice upon the subject. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Certain cases N. C.
