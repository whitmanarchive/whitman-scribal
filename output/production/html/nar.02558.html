Nov. 6, 1871. Messrs. Little Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: At the request of Assistant Attorney General Hill, I hereby acknowledge the receipt of you letter of the 16th ultimo, and also the books therein referred to. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
