June 25, 1869. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a copy of the deposition of Mr. Evariste Laroche, Chargé D'Affaires and Consul General of Hayti, in the matter of the information against the Gunboat Florida—which I will thank you to return to this office when no longer needed. Very Respectfully yours, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney Gen'l.
