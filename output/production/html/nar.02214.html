Dec. 1, 1871. Hon. Noah Davis, U.S. Attorney, New York. Sir: For special reasons, made known to me by the Treasury Department, I have thought proper to retain the Hon. Edwards Pierrepont, as a special assistant to you in the prosecution of Joshua D. Miner in the Circuit Court of your District, and enclose a commission which you will deliver to Mr. Pierrepont. I learn that this appointment is acceptable to you. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
E. Pierrepont retained
Special Ass't U.S. Atty.
