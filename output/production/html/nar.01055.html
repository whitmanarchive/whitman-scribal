October 28th, 1869. J. F. Breckinridge, Esq. San Jose, Cal. Sir: In reply to your application of the 17th inst., I am directed to say that it is against the rules of this Department to return papers placed upon its files. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
return of doc. declined.
