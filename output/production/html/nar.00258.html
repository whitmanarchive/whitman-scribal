October 30, 1867. Hon. Wm. R. Gorslin, U. S. Judge, Colorado Terr. Sir: Your letter of the 22d inst. requesting leave of absence from your Territory, is received, and I am directed by the Attorney General to say that four months leave of absence is granted you from the 15th of November next. Very respectfully, Your obd't serv't, F. U. Stitt, Act'g Chief Clerk.
