March 11, 1868. N. W. Trimble, Esq. Clerk of the Circuit Court United States, Mobile, Ala. Sir: I herewith transmit mandates from the Supreme Court to the Circuit Court of the United States, for the Southern District of Alabama, in the cases respectively of United States, vs. Stewart, and United States, vs Le Baron—and have to request you to acknowledge receipt of the same. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
recp't ack. March 28 '68.
