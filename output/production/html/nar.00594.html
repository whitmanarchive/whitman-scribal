July 7, 1868. Hon. J. M. Schofield Secretary of War. Sir: Your communication of 2d inst. addressed to the Secretary of State, enclosing Reports of alleged hostile organizations preparing to violate the Territory of Mexico, having been communicated to me, I have issued, to the District Attorney and Marshal at New Orleans, the instructions of which I have the honor herewith to enclose a copy. Respectfully, your obedient serv't. OH Browning​ O. H. Browning​ Attorney General ad interim
