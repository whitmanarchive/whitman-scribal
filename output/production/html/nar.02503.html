Oct. 7, 1871. Hon. J. H. Howe, Chief Justice, Cheyenne, W. T. Sir: Your resignation was laid before the President this morning, and it has been accepted by him, to take effect on the 31st instant. Very respectfully, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l & Acting Attorney General.
