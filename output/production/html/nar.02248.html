Dec. 14, 1871. A. B. Maynard Esq. U. S. Attorney, Detroit, Mich. Sir: I enclose copies of communications from Major O. M. Poe to the Light House Board, and of Peter Brown, Saginaw Bay Light House Keeper, to Major Poe, in relation to trespasses upon the rights of the United States at that station. Will you inquire, &c. [See letter p.92 preceding.]
trespasses, Mich.
