November 15, 1870. Hon. Caleb Cushing, Washington, D. C. Sir: I herewith enclose to you copies of a letter of the 12th inst., received from the Hon. Secretary of State—and of a letter to him from Mr. Silliman, of Counsel for the Trustees of Fraser, Trenholm, & Company. I respectfully request that you will prepare the forms of the judgments by the time Mr. Silliman arrives in Washington—so that if possible, this matter may be brought to a speedy termination. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General. (copies enc.)
