May 18, 1869. D. W. Middleton, Clerk U. S. Sup. Court. Sir: Suit No. 88 - Dec. 7, 1868, Wm. S. Grant, appellant, vs. U. S. Please inform me whether this case is decided or is pending - and, if decided, please send me a copy of the opinion of the Court. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk
