March 14, 1871. Alfred Hoss, Esq. Westminster, Carrol Co. Md. Sir: In compliance with your request of this morning, I herewith return to you your papers on file in this office, in support of your application for a position. Very respectfully, your obt servt, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
certain papers returned.
