Aug. 11, 1871. T. H. Talbot, Esq. Assistant Attorney General. Sir: In pursuance of the recommendation in your letter of the 10th instant I authorize the withdrawal of the appeal in the case of U.S. appellant, vs. Philip Djilinski and Davis Greenfield, appeal from the Court of Claims, No 2662 on the docket of Court of Claims, and you will prepare for my signature the necessary papers. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
C. of C. case.
