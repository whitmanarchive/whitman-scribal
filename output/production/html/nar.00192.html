January 26, 1867. Philip H. Morgan, Esq. U. S. Attorney, New Orleans, La. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney Gen. to trasmit​ you the enclosed petition of P. Alexander Lambert, who intervened in the case of the U. S. vs. the Estate of P. S. Bouford, with instructions to take any action in the matter you may deem expedient for the interest of all parties. Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
