July 23, 1870. Mr. Andrew J. Rodgers, Box 364, Washington, D. C. Sir: Your communication of July 8, 1870, addressed to the President, has been referred by him to me. In reply, I have to say that your proffered services in behalf of the Government are not desired. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
services declined
