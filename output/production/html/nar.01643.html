January 17, 1871. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: In accordance with the request in your letter of to-day, I return to you your letter of the 13th instant, and its enclosures, relating to a supposed crime by Thaddeus Roberts. No action had been taken by this Department in the matter. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Thad. Roberts's case.
