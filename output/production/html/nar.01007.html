September 30, 1869. Hon. J. W. Douglass, Act'g. Commissioner Internal Rev. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 29th instant, I have the honor to inform you that there are no affidavits on file in this Department touching the character and official conduct of James Marr, supervisor of Internal Revenue for Missouri. Very respectfully, Your obedient servant, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
character, &c. of James Marr, of Int. Rev.
