November 23, 1867. Charles E. Mix, Esq. Acting Commissioner of Indian affairs. Sir: For your information I enclose herewith a copy of the Attorney General's letter of yesterday, addressed to the U. S. Attorney for the District of New Mexico, for his instruction in relation to the Pueblo and the Camanche Indians of that Territory. Respectfully, your obd't servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l. [copy enc.]
