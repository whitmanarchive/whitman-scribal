January 18, 1870. Hon. Geo. W. French, Chief Justice, Yankton, Dakota T. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to say that you are allowed a leave of absence for thirty days from February 1, 1870, as requested in your letter of the 4th instant. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Cl'k.
Leave of absence, (30 days)
