Aug. 18, 1871. T. J. Durant, Esq. Washington, D. C. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 10th inst., and in compliance with your request, to enclose herewith the papers in relation to the claim of Mrs. Randolph, which were transmitted to this Department with your letter of May last. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
Mrs. Randolph's claim
Cal.
papers returned
