Attorney General's Office, December 15, 1865. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. 535 Chestnut Street, Philadelphia, Penn. Gents: Enclosed please find Twelve Dollars, ($12), the amount of your bills against this office. Very Respectfully, Wm. Stewart Chief Clerk. [$12, enclosed.]
