April 17, 1868. Messrs. Little, Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: Please find herewith a check on the Assistant Treasurer at New York for One hundred and ten 25/100 Dollars, the amount of your bill this day received—the receipt of which you are requested to acknowledge. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
