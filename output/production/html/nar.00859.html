April 2, 1869. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chairman Jud. Committee U. S. Senate. Sir: In accordance with the request of your Committee of this date, I have the honor to transmit the recommendations of Aubrey H. Smith, Esq. for U. S. Attorney for the Eastern District of Penn'a. You will please to return them when no longer required. Very resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney Gen. I invite your special attention to letters of Hon. J. Cadwalader, Judge of the U. S. Dist. Court of Penn'a, Eastern District, of Hon. R. S. Field, Judge of the U. S. for N. J. and of Wm M. Meredith, Esq. of Phil'a. [Enclosures as above.]
