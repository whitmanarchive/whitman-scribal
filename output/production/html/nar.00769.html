February 6, 1869. Hon. C. P. Clever, Delegate from New Mexico, H. of Representatives, Wash. Sir: I transmit herewith a leave of absence granted at your request to John Pratt, Esq. U. S. Marshal for the Territory of New Mexico, for ninety days. Resp. M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk. [Leave of absence for 90 days enc.]
