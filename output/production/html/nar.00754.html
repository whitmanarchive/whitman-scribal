January 23, 1869. Hon. Wm. Fullerton, No. 11 Pine st. New York. Sir: In answer to your letter of the 21st inst. I am instructed by the Attorney General to say that he addressed the U. S. Attorney, Mr. Courtney, on the 2d inst. relative to the subject about which you inquire, and to send you a copy of his letter, which you will find enclosed. Very respectfully, W. F. Peddrick.
