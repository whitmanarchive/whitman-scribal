July 3, 1869. Hon. Geo. M. Robeson, Secretary of the Navy. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a letter received this morning from Francis C. Barlow Esq. U. S. Marshal for the Southern Dist. of New York. Very Respectfully, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
