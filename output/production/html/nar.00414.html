January 29, 1868 S. M. Johnson, Esq. 326 H. st. Present. Sir: The Attorney General hopes, at an early day, to be able to take up for determination the case arising under the Acts of 1849 and 1864, which covers the one named in your notes of the 22d instant. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
