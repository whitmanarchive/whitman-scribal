June 17, 1868. A. B. Magruder, Esq. Attorney at Law, 36 St Paul street, Baltimore, Md. Sir: In response to your communication of the 15th instant, relative to the case of the United States, vs. John C.Braine​ Brain​ , I enclose a copy of our instructions of this date to the United States District Attorney. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
