May 10, 1869. Messrs. Little, Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: The Attorney General requests that you will forward this office the following books: Supplements of General Statutes of Massachusetts, from 1860 to 1868, both inclusive. Campbell's Lives of Lords Chancellor's Brougham and Lyndhurst, 8 vo.— Benjamin on Sales. Hoffman's California Land cases. Metcalf on contracts. Redfield on the Law of Railways—2 vols. Avery & Hobbs' Bankrupt Act. Conkling's Executive Powers. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
