May 4, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the papers which accompanied your letter of the 1st inst. touching a suit pending in the U. S. District Court for Iowa, ag't August Kaiser, for an alleged violation of the Internal Revenue law, and to state that I concur in the recommendation of the Commissioner of Internal Revenue, that a compromise of this suit be made upon the terms proposed. I am Sir, with great respect, &c. W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
