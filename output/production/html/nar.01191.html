February 8, 1870. J. H. Cole, Esq. 44 N. Water st. Philadelphia. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 7th inst. I am directed by the Attorney General to say that he has rendered his opinion on the Bounty Law of March 3, 1869, to the Secretary of the Treasury. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
