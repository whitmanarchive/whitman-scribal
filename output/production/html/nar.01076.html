November 15, 1869. Wm. Ware Peck, Esq. No. 29 Wall street, (Room 12,) New York. Sir: All applications are open to consideration. The information you speak of as coming from Washington, has, so far as I am aware, no truth in it whatever. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
