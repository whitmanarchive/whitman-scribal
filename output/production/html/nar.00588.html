July 2, 1868. Hon. T. B. Florence, Present. Sir: In compliance with your request on behalf of the party, and of other gentlemen who have recommended him to favor, I have consented to suspend the case of Christian Priesendantz for a few days—but enclose for your information a copy of my letter of this date to Mr. Ellmaker, the United States Marshal. Respectfully, your obd't serv't, OH Browning​ O. H. Browning​ Attorney General adinterim.
