May 3, 1869. Hon. John A. Rawlins Secretary of War. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a letter from Gen. H. Hand, Esq. U. S. Dist. Attorney for Dakotah Terr. dated the 28th ult., and to request that you will cause him to be furnished with a copy of the map therein referred to. Very Resp. Sir, &c. &c. W. A. Field, Act'g Attorney Gen.
