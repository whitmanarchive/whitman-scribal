Dec. 11, 1871. William Stone, Esq. Charleston, S. C. Sir: You are hereby appointed Assistant to the District Attorney for South Carolina with a salary of Two thousand dollars, ($2000), per annum, to commence on the 14th instant. You will take and transmit to this Department the oath required of District Attorneys. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Appt'm't as Ass't Atty,
S. C.
