December 1, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: I transmit herewith transcripts of Record in the cases of U. S. v. Schooner Nonesuch and cargo—& same, v. Schooner Rising Dawn, and cargo. The Attorney General requests that you will let him know the numbers of these cases on the docket. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
