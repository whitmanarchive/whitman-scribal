October 5, 1869. Hon. J. D. Cox, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the information for the Biennial Register, called for in your letter of August 2d, 1869. Very Respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
Biennial Register.
