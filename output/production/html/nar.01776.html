March 11, 1871. Hon. O. F. Strickland, Asso. Justice of the Supreme Court. Salt Lake City, Utah Terr. Sir: The leave of absence from your Territory for the month of June, requested in your letter of the 6th instant, is hereby granted. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
