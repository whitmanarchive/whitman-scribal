January 5, 1871. Wm. Brewster, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court, New Mexico. Sir: I enclose herewith as requested in your letter of the 16th ultimo, a copy of the rules of the Supreme Court of the United States. Very Resp'lly, &c B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General & acting Attorney General.
sending rules.
