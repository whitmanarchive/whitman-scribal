March 30, 1868. Hon. H. Stanbery, Present. Sir: In accordance with your request I send you a duly certified copy of the Opinion of Attorney General Speed, dated April 25th, 1865, upon the "appointment of Assistant Assessors of Internal Revenue." Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley Assistant Attorney General.
