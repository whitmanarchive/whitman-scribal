Nov. 14, 1871. H. H. Wells, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Richmond, Va. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 11th instant, and in compliance with your request to enclose a copy of the printed circular issued to District Attorneys for their direction in making certificates to titles of land purchased by the U. S. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
