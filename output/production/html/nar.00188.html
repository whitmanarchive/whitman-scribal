January 16, 1867 Hon. J. P. Kidder, Associate Justice Sup. Court Dakota Ter. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney Gen. to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence for one month from 20th Jan. 1867, on application of Hon. W. A. Burleigh. Very respectfully, yours, &c M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
