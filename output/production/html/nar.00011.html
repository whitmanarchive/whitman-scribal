Attorney Gen'l's Office, August 2, 1865. Hon. W. H. Seward, Secretary of State: Sir: I am directed by the President to request that you cause a Commission to issue to William P. Partello, Esq. to be a Justice of the Peace, in and for the county of Washington, & Dist. of Columbia. I am sir very respectfully, Your obedient servant, J. Hubley Ashton, Acting Attorney General.
