Dec. 8, 1871. Hon. D. W. Mahur Acting First Auditor of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith, for your information, a copy of the letter of the Hon. Willard Hall, to the President, of the 6th inst. resigning the office of District Judge of the United States for the District of Delaware. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General, & Acting Attorney General.
