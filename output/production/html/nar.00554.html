June 5, 1868. Hon. J. M. Scofield​ Schofield​ , Sir: Responding to your letter of the 1st instant, I herewith enclose a copy of ours of this date, in the case of Mr. Snow. Respectfully, your obd't serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
