March 25, 1870. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. Philadelphia, Penn. Gentlemen: Your letter of the 23d inst. advising this office of the transmission by express of 8 volumes of the English "Law Reports," and also your bill therefor, has been received, together with the books mentioned. I am directed to revoke the order heretofore given you for these Reports, and to request you to send no more of them to this office until otherwise ordered. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
