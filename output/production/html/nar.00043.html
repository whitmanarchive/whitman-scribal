Attorney General's Office. August 21, 1865. Sir: I am directed by the President to request that you cause a Commission to be issued to John L Williamson to be United States Attorney for the Western District of Tennessee. I am sir, vy Res. your obd't serv't James Speed, Attorney General Sec. of State.
