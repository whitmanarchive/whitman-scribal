May 20, 1868. Clarence A. Seward, Esq. Counsellor at Law, Box 217, New York. Sir: I enclose herewith a copy of our letter of this date, calling upon the District Attorney for a supplemental Report in the matter of Morris, referred to in your favor of the 15th instant, and previous correspondence. Respectfully, &c. John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
