December 20, 1870. Smith C. Young, Esq. Kasse, Limestone Co. Texas. Sir: As requested in your letter of the 15th instant, I return herewith the papers filed in this Department in support of your application for the position of Marshal of The United States for Wyoming. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l, and Acting Attorney General.
papers returned.
