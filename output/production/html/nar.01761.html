March 7, 1871. Theodore N. Melvin, Esq. No. 117 Broadway, New York. Sir: The case of Augustus C. Radcliffe, referred to in your letter of the 6th instant, has not been presented to this office, and I am therefore unable to give you any information upon the subject. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
