March 25, 1870 Messrs. Little, Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: I have to request you to furnish this office with a complete set of the English edition of the "Law Reports," including all the series, together with the statutes, from the commencement of their publication in 1865, to the present time. Please send, also, if you have any such to spare, a late catalogue of French law books. Very Resp. your ob't serv't, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
