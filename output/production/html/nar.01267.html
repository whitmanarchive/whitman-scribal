March 22, 1870. Geo. C. Corning, Esq. President of the Topeka Bank, Topeka, Kansas. Sir: Your letter of the 8th instant has been received. The matter to which you refer therein has been duly attended to. Pursuant to your request I herewith return the check contained in your letter. Very respectfully E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
letter acknowledged.
