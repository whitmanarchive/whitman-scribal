Aug. 7, 1871. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: I enclose copies of communications from the Assistant Attorney General having charge of business in the Court of claims, in reference to the claim of Messrs. Howell, & Rice, to which you called my attention. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
