Nov. 13, 1871. Hon. George P. Fisher, U. S. Attorney, Washington, D. C. Sir: I transmit for your information the copy of a letter from R. McPhail Smith, Esq. District Attorney for the Middle District of Tennessee, in relation to the case of Strohmier and Hatfield. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
