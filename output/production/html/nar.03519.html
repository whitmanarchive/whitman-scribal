Dec. 12, 1871. Hon. George S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: In pursuance of the request in your letter of the 11th inst., the District Attorney for the District of Columbia has been directed to appear for the defence in the case of Chauncey C. Ryder vs. Columbus Delano, late Commissioner of Internal Revenue. Very respectfully, &c A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
