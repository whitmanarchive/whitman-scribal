January 17, 1870. Messrs. Little, Brown, & Co. Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: The books forwarded by you to this office per express, of which you advised us in your letter of the 13th inst, have been received; and in reply to your inquiry, I have to state that we desire you to send the balance of the order heretofore given you, consisting of the Reports mentioned in your letter, as you may be able to collect them from time to time. I am, with great respect, Your obd't serv't, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
