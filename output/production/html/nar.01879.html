May 24, 1871. F. H. Mays, Esq. Fincastle, Va. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter without date, and to say that this Department issues no instructions to U. S. Commissioners. I send you copies of Instructions to Dist. Attorneys and Marshals, from which you may possibly derive some information. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
