Aug. 22, 1871. H. W. Bingham, Esq. Treasurer Union Republican State Central Comm. Philadelphia Penn. Sir: In answer to your letter of the 19th inst. I have the honor to inform you that the names and address of the Registers in Bankruptcy are not recorded in this office. You can probably obtain them, and the information which you desire concerning the compensation which the Register in Bankruptcy receive, from the U.S Marshals of your State. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
