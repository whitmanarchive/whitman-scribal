Feb. 27, 1869. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the acc't of John McKeon, Esq. approved by me, and amounting to Five hundred dollars, for legal services rendered in the case of the U. S. ag't The Estate of George A. Gardner, and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very Resp. Yours, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney Gen'l.
Acc't of John McKeon
