January 11, 1868. Hon. B. F. Wade, President of the Senate, pro tempore Sir: In accordance with a Resolution of the Senate of the United States, adopted January 8th, 1868, I have the honor to transmit, herewith, copies of the Opinions officially given by me in relation to the pay of retired and reserved officers of the Navy. With high respect, your obedient servant, Henry Stanbery Attorney General.
