Feb. 26, 1869. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior Sir: I herewith return the papers submitted with your letter of the 11th inst. containing the adjudication of the Commissioner of the General Law office in the matter of the suspended entries of certain lands at East Laginaw, Mich., by Charles Rodd and Henry Peter, which has received my approval. Very Resp. Yours, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney Gen'l.
