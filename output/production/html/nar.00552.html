June 5, 1868. Hon. A. Flanders, House of Representatives. Sir: The Attorney General ad interim directs me to say that before granting an extension of leave of absence to Judge Hewitt, he desires that his application for that extension should be put on file in this office. Respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
