January 23, 1868. J. W. McCorkle, No. 221 Pennsylvania avenue, Washington, D. C. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that the correspondence, a copy of which is requested in your letter of the 22d instant, does not appear of record in this office. Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
