April 30, 1869. Hon. A. E. Borie, Secretary of the Navy. Sir: I have received the enclosed letter from Mr. John A. Bolles, Solicitor, etc.; but it does not appear whether or not it was written by your direction. I will be happy to learn whether you have any desire in regard to the subject matter of this communication. Very respectfully yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
