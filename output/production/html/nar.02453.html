Sept. 8, 1871. Charles Bradshaw, Esq. Montgomery, Ala. Sir: I have this morning telegraphed you that if you report for duty on the 15th instant, you will be appointed to a $1200 position in this Department. Owing to the great press of business on me, I desire, if you accept the position, that you will be punctual in reporting on that date, or earlier if possible. I wish to say that when not engaged as an amanuensis, you will be required to perform other clerical duty​ , principally copying. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
