Feb. 16, 1871. Hon. Alexander Rives, U. S. Judge, Western Dist. Virginia. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a commission appointing you to be District Judge of the United States for the Western Dist. of Virginia. You will be pleased to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l
