April 19, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. disbursing Clerk, Treasury Dept. for the sum of $4821:57, and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very resp. sir, y'r obt sv't, E. R. Hoar, Attorney Gen.
Salaries of the Att. Gen. Asst. Att. Gen. Clerks & Messenger, for April, 1869..... $2821:57
Contingent Expenses........ 1500:
Legal Assistance & other necessary expenditures in the disposal of private land claims in California..... 500:
$4821:57
