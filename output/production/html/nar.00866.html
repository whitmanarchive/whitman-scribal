April 10, 1869. Hon. Hamilton Fish Secretary of State. Sir: I am in receipt of a letter from you of the 3d inst. stating certain information received by your Department from the Charge d'Affaires of Haiti, relative to the fitting out of a steamer named "Florida" at Chester, Penn. for the service of the insurgents now in arms against his Government. I enclose you herewith a copy of a communication addressed to the United States Attorney for the Eastern Dist. of Penn. upon this subject. Very resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney Gen.
