January 16, 1867. E. C. Carrington, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Washington D. C. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, the enclosed letter is referred to you for consideration whether it is advisable to subpoena this man Blake, & get the name of the witness upon examination of Blake. Very Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
