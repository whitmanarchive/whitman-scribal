Oct. 26, 1871. Hon. Moses Hallet, Chief Justice, Denver City, Colorado. Sir: By direction of the President, the leave of absence asked for in your letter of the 14th ultimo for the month of October is hereby granted. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General, & Acting Attorney General.
