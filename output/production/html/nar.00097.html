Attorney General's Office, April 11, 1866. To A. G. Stevens, Esq. U. S. Deputy Marshal, Buffalo, N. Y. Sir: I have your letter of the 7th of April, 1866, in regard to the suit against you by Mrs. Dibble for the property you occupy in the city of Buffalo. I understand from your letter that the proceeding is against the Postmaster, Custom House Officer, & Marshal. You will call upon the District Attorney, & pursue his advice in the matter I am, Sir, most respectfully, James Speed Attorney General.
