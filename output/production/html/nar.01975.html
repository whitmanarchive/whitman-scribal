July 13, 1869. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I am directed by the President to request you to issue Commissions appointing William H. Chase, Samuel W. Pearson, James C. Dulin, Alexander Garden, John Cornell, and Erastus C. Weaver, to be Justices of the Peace for the District of Columbia. Very Respectfully, &c. &c. E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
