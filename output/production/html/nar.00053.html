Attorney General's Office, August 24, 1865. To the Hon. Wm. H. Gale, & the Hon. Ch. F. Holly. Gentlemen: This is to inform you that the Hon. Wm. H. Gale has been appointed as the successor of Judge Charles Lee Armor; and the Hon. Ch. F. Holly the successor of Judge Allen A. Bradford, in the Territory of Colorado. I am, most respectfully, &c James Speed Attorney General. [Add. to Mr. Gale, Brooklyn, N. Y.]
