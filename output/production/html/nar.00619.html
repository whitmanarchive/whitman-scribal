July 31, 1868. Hon. W. H. Hooper, Care of Scott & Dash, 112 Pearl street, New York City. Dear Sir: In accordance with your request, I transmit herewith the commissions of Chief Justice Wilson, and Associate Justice Hoge, of the Supreme Court of your Territory. Be pleased to acknowledge their receipt, & give me the addresses of the Judges, if you have them. Very respectfully, yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
