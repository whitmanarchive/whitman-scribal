May 20, 1869. Blanton Duncan, Esq. Louisville, Ky. Sir: A letter relating to the suit now pending in the Circuit Court of Ky. ag't. certain real property in which you claim an interest has been sent to the Attorney of the U. S. for the Dist. of Kentucky.—This letter will be shown to you by him on your request to see it. Very Resp. yours, W. A. Field, Ass't. Attorney Gen'l.
