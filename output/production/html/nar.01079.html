November 16, 1869. Hon. Giles A. Smith, 2d Assistant P. M. Gen'l. Sir: In response to your request of yesterday, I have the honor to transmit herewith a list of the several District Attorneys, with their post office address. Very respectfully, Your ob't serv't, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
List sent.
