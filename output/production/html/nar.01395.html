June 25, 1870. Messrs. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. Philadelphia, Penn. Gentlemen: Your letter of the 23d isnt. has been received. You are requested to furnish Vols. 13 to 22, inclusive, of Texas Reports; but not Vol. 24. As to the new edition of Rawle's Reports, can you not supply Vols. 4 and 5, without the others, at nearly corresponding rates? Very respectfully, your obd't serv't, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Cl'k.
