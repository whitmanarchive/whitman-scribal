May 26, 1871. Hon. Leonard Myers, Philadelphia, Penn. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, I transmit to you the papers filed in this Department in support of the application of Judge Thayer for the office of Circuit Judge of the United States. Be pleased to acknowledge their receipt. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
