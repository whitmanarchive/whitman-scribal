July 13, 1870. Hon. B. F. Butler, House of Representatives. Sir: I have received your letter of the 12th inst. asking for a copy of the Opinion of the Attorney General in the case of R. W. Gibbes, given to the Secretary of State. I have asked the Secretary of State whether he has any objection to my sending you a copy of the opinion. If he has none, I will have it immediately copied and sent to you. Very respectfully, your obd't serv't A. T. Akerman, Attorney General
request for opinion in Gibbes's case.
