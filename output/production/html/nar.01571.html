December 22, 1870. Hon. J. W. Douglass, Acting Comm'r Internal Revenue. Sir: Your letter of the 20th instant, enclosing a copy of a letter from District Attorney Hazelton, in relation to forwarding the mandates in certain cases, mentioned, has been received; and I have to-day written to Mr. Hazelton that I shall move that the mandates be issued as soon as the rules of the Court will allow. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General, and Acting Attorney General.
