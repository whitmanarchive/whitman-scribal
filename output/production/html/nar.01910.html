June 10, 1869. Henry W. Moulton, Esq. Boise City, Idaho Terr. Sir: I have this day forwarded your commission, (duplicate) as U. S. Marshal for Idaho, to "Hon. David Noggle, Chief Justice, Boise City, Idaho T."—you will please acknowledge the receipt of it as soon as you qualify. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk.
