October 11, 1869. Hon. John H. Howe, Chief Justice, Cheyenne, Wyoming T. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence from Nov. 15th 1869, to Feb'y 1st, 1870, in accordance with the request contained in your letter of the 6th inst. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Leave of absence.
