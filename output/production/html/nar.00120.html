October 3, 1866. To Hon. O. H. Browning. Secretary of the Interior. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, I have the honor to transmit herewith two accounts, approved by him, & amounting in the aggregate to seven hundred & fifty dollars ($750.00) of L. H. Bond, for legal services as assistant to R. M. Corwine, Esq. U. S. Attorney for the Southern Dist. of Ohio, for the quarters ending June 30th, & Sept. 30th 1866; and to request their payment out of the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, &c M. F. Pleasants, Act'g Ch'f. Clerk.
