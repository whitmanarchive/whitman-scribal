February 7, 1871 Hon. S. P. Morrill, House of Representatives. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 4th inst. and in compliance with your request to enclose herewith a list of the Circuit Judges of the United States, and their residences. Very respectfully, &c, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
