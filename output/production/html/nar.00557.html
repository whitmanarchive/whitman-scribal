June 8, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk United States Supreme Court. Sir: Please furnish this Office, at your earliest convenience, with the remainder of the opinions of the Supreme Court, delivered during December term, 1867, as far as they are printed. Very respectfully, your obd't serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
