December 30, 1867. Hon. Edwards Pierrepont, Counsellor at Law, New York. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith, for your information, by direction of the Attorney General, a copy of a letter just received from Edward C. Carrington, Esq. United States Attorney for the District of Columbia. Respectfully, Your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l. [copy enc.]
