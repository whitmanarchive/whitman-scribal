Attorney General's Office. August 14, 1865. To the Secretary of State. Sir: I herewith return the letter of Mr. B. O. Duncan, which I have read. I fear that Judge O'Neal is too old—will bear his name in mind. I have the honor to be, Respectfully yours, &c. James Speed Attorney General.
