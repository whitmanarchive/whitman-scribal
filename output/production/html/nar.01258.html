March 19, 1870. Hon. George S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of $5934:23/100, and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very respectfully, your ob't serv't, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General. Salaries of the Attorney General, Assistant Attorneys General, Clerks, & Messenger—for March, 1870 - - $2934:23. Contingent expenses - - - - - - - - 2000: Purchase of Law, & other necessary books - 1000: $5934:23
