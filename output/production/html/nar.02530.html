Oct. 25, 1871. John D. Van Buren, Esq. private Secretary, &c. Albany, N. Y. Sir: I am directed by the Solicitor General and Acting Attorney Gen'l to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 23d instant, transmitting a copy of the "Report of the Secretary of State on Criminal Statistics" in the State of New York, and to thank you for the same. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
