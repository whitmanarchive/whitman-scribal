November 14, 1868. D. Marvin Esq. My dear Sir: In reply to your note of 12th inst., desiring, as counsel for the Commercial Nav'g'n Co., an interview with me during my expected visit to New York, I beg to say that I shall be pleased to see you there, but can make no more definite appointment until I reach the city. Yours respectfully, Wm W. Evarts, Attorney General
