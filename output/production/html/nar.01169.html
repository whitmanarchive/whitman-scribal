January 22, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith for your information a letter from the U. S. Attorney for the Northern District of New York, dated the 20th inst. enclosing a copy of a letter from Sumner L. Hazen, Deputy U. S. Marshal, in relation to Fenian movements in that District. I will thank you to return these papers. Very resp. your obd't servt, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General. (enc.)
