Sept. 27, 1866. To A. F. Campbell, Esq. U. S. Marshal, Brooklyn, N. Y. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General, in reply to your letter of the 25th inst. that your resignation of the office of U. S. Marshal for the Eastern District of New York, tendered on the 25th ult. & which had previously escaped his attention, is hereby accepted. Very respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Act'g. Ch'f. Clerk.
