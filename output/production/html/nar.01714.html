Feb. 11, 1871. The President, Sir: The enclosed application of Mr. Haskins for a military cadetship for his son, has been referred to me by your order. I think that this reference must have been made by mistake, as this Department has no connection with such appointments; and therefore I respectfully return the application. With great respect, I am, Sir, Your obd't serv't, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
