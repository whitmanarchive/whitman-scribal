January 24, 1871. Hon. Wm. T Jones, Asso. Justice, Sup. Court, Wyoming Terr. Washington, D. C. Sir: Your leave of absence is extended, as requested in your letter of the 23d instant. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
