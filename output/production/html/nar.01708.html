Feb. 11, 1871. Hon. Hiram Knowles, Asso. Justice, Sup. Ct, Montana Terr. Keokuk, Iowa. Sir: In compliance with your request, transmitted through Mr. McCrary, your leave of absence is extended until the 15th of April, 1871. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
