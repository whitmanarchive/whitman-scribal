Nov. 7, 1871. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I think it my duty to transmit for your information the copy of an unofficial letter which I have just received from Andrew Sloan, Esq. Assistant district Attorney in Georgia, in relation to the indictment against Mr. Robb, the Collector at Savannah. Mr. Sloan is a man of intelligence and integrity, and I fully rely on his statements. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
