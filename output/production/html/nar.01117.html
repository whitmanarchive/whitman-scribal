December 18, 1869. A. M. Clapp, Esq. Congressional Printer. Sir: I will thank you to have printed, as soon as possible, the Record in the case of Jacob D. Cox, Secretary of the Interior, vs. U. S. ex rel William McGarrahan, as the case has been assigned for argument early in January, and the Record is needed for use. Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Assistant Attorney General.
