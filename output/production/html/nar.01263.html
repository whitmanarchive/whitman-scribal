March 22, 1870. Publisher of "the Nation." Box 6732, New York City. Enclosed you will find Five dollars for twelve months subscription of this Office to "the Nation," to Feb. 23d, 1871. I will thank you to send me a receipt in duplicate. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
