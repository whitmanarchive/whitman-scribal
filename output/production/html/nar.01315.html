April 12, 1870. Messrs. Beals, Greene & Co. Boston Post Building, Boston. Gentlemen: Please find enclosed a check for ten dollars for subscription of this Office to the "Boston Post" from Sept. 16, 1869, to Sept. 16, 1870. I send also the bill for receipt. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk. (enc's)
"Boston Post"
