April 29, 1868. Hon. T. C. McCreery, United States Senate. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a copy of our letter of this date to the Secretary of the Treasury, in the case of Messrs. Mobberly and Monarch. With the greatest respect, &c. John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
