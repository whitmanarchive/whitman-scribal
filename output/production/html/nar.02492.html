Sept. 27, 1871. Messrs. Lewis & Fullerton, Washington, D. C. Gentlemen: This Department is in receipt of your letter of the 28th inst., and, in compliance with your request, I have this day sent to Col. Spalding copies of the Ku Klux laws. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
