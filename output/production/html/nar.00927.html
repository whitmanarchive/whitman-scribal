May 14, 1869. Franklin Fraser, Esq. Clerk U. S. Dist. Court. Fernandina, Fla. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, I transmit herewith the mandate of the U. S. Supreme Court in the case of The U. S. vs. Jasper Strong, and ask that it be filed in the Court. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
