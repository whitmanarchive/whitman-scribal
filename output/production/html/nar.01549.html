December 16, 1870. Hon. Charles D. Drake, U. S. Senate. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith your Commission for the office of Chief Justice of the Court of Claims. You will be pleased to send to this Department a copy of your official oath. Very respectfully, &c, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor General & Acting Attorney General.
Commission sent.
