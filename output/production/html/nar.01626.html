July 13, 1870. Frank M. Pixley, and L. D. Latimer, Esqrs. San Francisco, California. Sirs: On account of my absence from Washington, your letter of the 6th ult, in relation to the case of Pickett, vs. Hastings and others, and your claim for a retainer for services therein, has just been brought to my notice. Your claim for fees will receive due attention. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
certain fees, (Cal.)
