November 23, 1867. E. C. Carrington, Esq. United States Attorney, Washington, D. C. Sir: In the matter of Brooke Mackall, Jr. vs. John C. McFerran and A. R. Blunt, rule to show cause why injunction should not issue as prayed, I enclose process and copies of papers received from the War Department. The Attorney General directs you, in conformity to the request of the Secretary of War, ad interim, to appear & defend the interests of the United States in the premises. The hearing is set for the 25th of this month, in the Supreme Court of the District of Columbia. Respectfully, &c. John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
