Sept. 22, 1871. Hon. J. H. Howe, Chief Justice, Cheyenne, ?Wyoming Washington Terr. Sir: I enclose herewith for your information and explanation a copy of a letter addressed to this Department under date of the 18th instant, by P. S. Wilson, Esq. relative to your absence from your post. Very respectfully, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l, & Acting Attorney
