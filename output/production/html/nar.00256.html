October 25, 1867. Chas. A. de Blessier, Esq. Burlingham, Sullivan Co. N. Y. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 12th inst. I am instructed to say that your case is one for the action of the Courts, and not the Department. This office cannot advise you in the matter. Respectfully, F. U. Stitt, Pardon Clerk.
