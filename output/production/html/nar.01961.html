July 6, 1869. Hon. John A. Rawlins, Secretary of War. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of a communication dated June 26th, 1869, from David W. Lewis, Secretary of the Georgia Agricultural Society, relative to the "Laboratory property" at Macon, Ga., handed to the Attorney General by the President. Very Respectfully, W. A. Field, Assistant Attorney General.
