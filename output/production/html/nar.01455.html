November 5, 1870. Hon. C. Delano, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: In accordance with your request of this date, I have the honor to transmit herewith the papers filed in this Department in support of the application for the appointment of William Blakewell as Circuit Judge of the United States for the Third Circuit. Very respectfully, &c. A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
