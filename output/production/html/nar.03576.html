Dec. 21, 1871. Hon. L. D. Shoemaker, H. of R, Washington, D.C. Sir: After a careful examination of the case of Richard Barrett for whom you have so earnestly urged an application for pardon, I feel bound to decline recommending one at present. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
