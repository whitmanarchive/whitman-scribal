Nov. 24, 1871. C. K. Davis, Esq. U. S. Attorney, St. Paul, Minn. Sir: Your letter of the 19th instant, giving an account of the cases against O'Neill and Curley is received. As at present advised, this Department is entirely satisfied with your conduct in the matter. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
