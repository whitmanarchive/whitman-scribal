March 13, 1869. Hon. J. D. Cox, Secretary of the Interior, Sir: I have the honor to return to your Department, herewith, the adjudication of the Commissioner of the General Land Office, & other papers, in certain cases of suspended land entries of Henry McGee and Philip Biddel, in the San Francisco district, California, which were transmitted to this office by your predecessor under cover of a letter dated the 13th ult. The action of the Commissioner is concurred in by me. Very Resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
