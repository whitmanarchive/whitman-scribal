November 21, 1868. Hon. Wm. H. Seward, Secretary of State, Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to transmit, for the information of your Department the enclosed Report relative to rumored expeditions for the invasion of Cuba, communicated to this office by the U. S. Attorney for the Southern Dist. of New York,— & also a copy of his accompanying letter. Very Res. your ob't serv't. W. F. Peddrick, Acting Chief Clerk.
