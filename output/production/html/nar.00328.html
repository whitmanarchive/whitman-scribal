December 5, 1867. General U. S. Grant, U. S.A. Secretary of War, ad interim Sir: I have the honor to return herewith all the papers relating to the public property at Harper's Ferry, which were transmitted by you to this office on the 11th of October last, in compliance with a request of the Attorney General of the 7th of same month. Very Respectfully, Your obd't serv't, John M. Binckley Assistant Attorney Gen'l. [papers returned.]
