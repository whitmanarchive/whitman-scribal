January 5, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of this date, enclosing a copy of a telegraphic communication from Mr. Roberts, the Spanish Minister, requesting the detention of the steamer "Anna," and to say that I have transmitted to the U. S. Attorney at Charleston, S. C. the instructions of which the enclosed is a copy. Very resp. your obd't serv't, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
Steamer Anna.
