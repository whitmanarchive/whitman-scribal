Approved Accounts, Forwarded to Secretary of Interior for pay for legal services of Assistants to United States District Attorneys. April 3d, 1868. The Honorable, The Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account approved by me, and amounting to Five hundred Dollars, ($500:) of John K. Valentine, for legal services as assistant to Charles Gilpin, Esq. United States Attorney for the Eastern District of Pennsylvania, for the quarter ending March 31st 1868, and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, your obedient serv't, O. H. Browning, Attorney General ad interim Approvals, of similar form to above, have been issued, in favor of the following named persons, at the dates, on account of services, and for the sums, specified:
