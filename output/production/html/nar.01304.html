April 5, 1870. R. S. Tharin, Esq. Attorney at Law, Charleston, S.C. Sir: Your complaint against Mr. Corbin, the U. S. Attorney for South Carolina, has been investigated, and from the facts which I have ascertained, I do not consider that it requires any further action on my part. Very respectfully, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
