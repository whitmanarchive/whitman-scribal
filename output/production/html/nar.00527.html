May 5, 1868. Louis Janin, Esq. Counsellor at Law, present Sir: In compliance with your request under date of 28th ultimo, I have instructed the District Attorney for the Northern District of Florida, under this date, to make all practicable search for the missing documents, and herewith enclose a copy of our instructions. Respectfully, your obed't serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
