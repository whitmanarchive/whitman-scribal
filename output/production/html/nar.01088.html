November 29, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: The enclosed letter from Hon. G. W. Scofield is respectfully referred to the Secretary of the Treasury, with the request that if he sees fit to act in the matter he will communicate directly with the Dist. Attorney having charge of the suit. Very respectfully, W. A. Field, Assistant Attorney General
Treasury case.
