Aug. 24, 1871. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: In answer to your letter of the 18th instant, I have the honor to say that in view of the value of the property, and the labor of investigating titles, I think that the account of the United States District Attorney for services in examining titles to lands in Omaha, which was enclosed in your letter, and which I return, is reasonable and just. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
acct of U.S. Att'y
Omaha.
