Nov. 7, 1871. G. N. Hitchcock, Esq. San Diego, Cal. Sir: Your letter of September 3d has been received. You will oblige me by furnishing promptly the brief you promised me in the case of Pio Pico, vs. The United States. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
