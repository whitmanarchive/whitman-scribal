March 1, 1870. Hon. John A. Bingham, Chairman Judiciary Committee House of Representatives. Sir: Pursuant to your request of the 26th ult., I have the honor to send enclosed herewith a copy of a letter of E. L. Gould to Attorney General Black, dated March 20, 1858. This, with the papers and records already sent you, is every thing that can be found in this office, of the matters you ask for. Very respectfully, [no handwritten text supplied here] Attorney General. (papers acc.)
