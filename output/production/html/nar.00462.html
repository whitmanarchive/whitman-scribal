March 11, 1868. Col. John H. Wheeler, Statistical Bureau, Treasury Department. Sir: I herewith enclose a copy of our letter of this date to Dr. S. J. Wheeler, of Windsor, N. C. Respectfully, yours, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l. [copy enc.]
