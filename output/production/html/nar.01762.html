March 7, 1871. J. W. Schaumburg, Esq. Philadelphia, Penn. Sir: I have caused search to be made in this office, and regret to inform you that no such papers as you have described in your letter of the 27th ult. have been found. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
no papers.
