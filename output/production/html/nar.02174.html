Nov. 16, 1871. John D. Pope, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Savannah, Geo. Sir: Your letter of the 14th inst. requesting certified copies of papers to be used as evidence in the case of The United States, vs. T. P. Robb, has been received. I have requested the Secretary of the Treasury to furnish you with certified copies. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
T. P. Robb's case.
