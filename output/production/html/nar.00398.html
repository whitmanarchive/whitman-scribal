January 10, 1868. Henry Jenkins, Esq. Charlottesville, Va. Sir: Your letter of the 28th ultimo to the President having been by him referred to the Attorney General, I herewith enclose a copy of our instructions of this date to the District Attorney. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
