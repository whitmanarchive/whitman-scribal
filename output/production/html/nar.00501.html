April 1, 1868. F. U. Stitt, Esq. Washington. Sir: By direction of the Attorney Gen'l ad interim, your resignation as Pardon Clerk in this office, this day received, is hereby accepted. Very respectfully Your obedient servant, M. F. Pleasants, Chief clerk.
