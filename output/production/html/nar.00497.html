March 30, 1868. C. T. Botts, Esq. New York av. and 15th street, Washington, D. C. Sir: For your information in the case of Dr. James Bolton, I enclose herewith a copy of our letter of this date to the District Attorney at New York. The papers submitted with your letter of the 20th instant, are all returned herewith. Respectfully, your obedient serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Genl.
