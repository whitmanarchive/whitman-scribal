July 5, 1870. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith, with my approval, the account of Albert H. Horton, Esq, U. S. Attorney for the District of Kansas, for services rendered in the case against Gen. G. M. Dodge, by request of the Secretary of War dated Oct. 27, 1869—amounting to 11100 Very Resp'y &c. E. R. Hoar, Attorney Gen'l.
bill for defence of Gen. Dodge.
