June 11, 1868 John Statz, Esq. Washington, D. C. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General adinterim to say that your letter of the 10th instant, proposing certain questions as to your right of citizenship, has been received and referred to the Secretary of State. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
