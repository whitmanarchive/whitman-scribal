June 17, 1870. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of $2808,38, and charge the same to the appropriations for the salaries of the of the​ Attorney General, Assistant Attorneys General, Clerks and Messenger of this Office, for June, 1870. Very respectfully, your obedient servant, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
