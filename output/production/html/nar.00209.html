April 20, 1867. Hon. Gideon Welles, Secretary of the Navy. Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the papers in the case of Rear Admiral Goldsborough, transmitted under cover of your letter of the 16th inst. Respectfully, &c. yours, Henry Stanbery Attorney General.
