March 3, 1869. John E. Ward, Esq. No. 61 Wall street New York City Sir: I enclose a duplicate original of the determination & order which I have made in the matter of Nott & Co's. claim ag't The U. S., the other original having been delivered to the State Department. Very resp. yours, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General
