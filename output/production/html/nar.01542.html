December 14, 1870. E. Wright Newman, Esq. No. 8½ Pearl street, Baltimore, Md. Sir: In answer to yours of the 13th instant, I inform you that there is no special mode for application for a District Attorneyship. I suggest that if you desire the District Attorneyship of Utah, you address to this office a written application, accompanied with such testimonials as you can obtain of your moral, political, and professional qualifications. It is desirable that such testimonials should come from well-known persons who are in political sympathy with this administration. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
letter answered.
