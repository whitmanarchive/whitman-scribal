September 23, 1871. J. H. Caldwell, Esq. La Grange, Georgia. Sir: This Department has received your letter of the 18th instant, enclosing receipted account, amounting to $325, for services rendered, and expenses incurred under the direction of the Attorney General. I transmit herewith a draft on New York for the amount of your account, and request that you will be pleased to acknowledge its receipt. Very respectfully, &c. A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
$325 sent
