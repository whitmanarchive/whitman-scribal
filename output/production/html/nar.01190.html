February 7, 1870. Hon. A. A. Sargent, House of Representatives. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 4th inst. the Attorney General directs me to inform you that the Commission of the Hon. Lorenzo Sawyer was sent to him at San Francisco, on the 21st ult. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
Com. of Lorenzo Sawyer, Cal.
