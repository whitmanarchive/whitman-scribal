February 1, 1869. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to $2,575.00 of Messrs. Sale & Dowd, special counsel employed to assist James E. Stewart, Esq. U. S. Attorney for the Northern District of Mississippi—& to request its payment from the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, &c. Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General.
