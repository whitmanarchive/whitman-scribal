Attorney General's Office. August 10, 1865. Hon. Wm. H. Seward, Secretary of state, Sir: I am directed by the President to request that you cause a Commission to issue to James Donaldson to be a Justice of the Peace in and for the county of Washington and District of Columbia. I am sir, very respectfully, Your obedient servant James Speed Attorney General.
