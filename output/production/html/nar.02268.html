Dec. 20, 1871. John A. Minnis, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Montgomery, Ala Sir: I acknowledge the receipt of your letter to the President of the 12th instant, on the subject of the Ku Klux outrages, &c. which the President has referred to me. As the communication does not seem to require any specific action, no further answer is necessary. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
