March 29, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to request that you will send to this office a transcript of the record in the case of Parley L. Howlett, Pl'ff in error vs. David T. Littler & al. N. 287. Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
trans of rec. requested.
