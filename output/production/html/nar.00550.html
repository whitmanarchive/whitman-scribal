June 3, 1868. Hon. John R. McBride, Chief Justice, Boise City, Idaho Terr. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General ad interim to say that the resignation of your office of Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Idaho Territory, tendered in your letter dated April 23d, 1868, has been accepted by the President. Very respectfully, your obedient servant, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
