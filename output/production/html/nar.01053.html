October 28, 1869. Charles G. Rumford, Esq. Clerk U. S. Courts, Wilmington, Del. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that having no official relations with the clerks of the United States Courts, this Office has issued no circulars or instructions for their guidance in the discharge of their official duties. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
