October 19, 1870. Hon. Jas. B. McKean, Chief Justice of Utah Territory, Salt Lake City. Sir: The leave of absence requested in your letter of the 12th inst., to wit. from the 1st of November to the 15th of December next, is granted. This leave is given with the understanding that no court over which you preside will regularly sit during that period. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
