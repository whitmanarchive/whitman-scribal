Sept. 29, 1871. Jacob Shroder, Esq. Cincinnati, Ohio. Sir: I have your letter of the 26th instant, enclosing your receipt for salary for the present month, and in compliance with your request have paid $100 to Saks & Co., and transmit herewith a draft on New York for the balance of $46.74, the receipt of which I will thank you to acknowledge. I return a receipt of the Baltimore & Ohio RR. Company, which was enclosed I presume inadvertently in your letter to me. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk
money paid &c.
