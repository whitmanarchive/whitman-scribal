April 14, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to transmit the enclosed motion in the case of the United States, vs. Koehler et al. to be filed—and to say that he will call it up on Friday, the 15th inst. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
motion, case of U. S. vs. Koehler.
