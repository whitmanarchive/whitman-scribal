June 15, 1869. Hon. A. E. Borie, Secretary of the Navy. Sir: I have the honor to inform you, in reply to your letter of April 17, 1869, that the mandate of the Supreme Court in the case of the steamer "Georgia," was sent to the Clerk of the U. S. Dist. Court at Boston, on the 14th of May last. Very Resp. your ob't servt, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
