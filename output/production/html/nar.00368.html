December 23, 1867. Messrs. Beals, Greene, & Co. "Boston Post." Please find enclosed Ten dollars, for your paper from September 16, 1867, to September 16, 1868, as per your bill of December 20, 1867. Very Respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
