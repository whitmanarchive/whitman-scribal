April 2, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court United States. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to transmit to you the enclosed notice given to the defendants in the case of The United States, vs. Blyew & Kennard—with the request that it be filed. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Notice, Blyew & Kennard's case.
