January 10, 1870. Hon. C. S. Eyster, Asso. Justice, Denver, Colorado T. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence from your District as requested in your letter of the 4th instant. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
Leave of absence.
