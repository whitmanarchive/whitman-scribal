December 13, 1869, Messrs. Rannay & Bolton, Cleveland, Ohio. Gentlemen: In reply to your inquiry of the 10th inst. the Attorney General directs me to say that the case of E. Bassett et al. vs. U. S. has been reached, argued, and submitted on briefs. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
