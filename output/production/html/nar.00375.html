December 28, 1867. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chairman Judiciary Committee, United States Senate. Sir: I have the honor to refer to a previous communication from this office of the 12th instant, and to enclose herewith a copy of another letter from William Dorsheimer, Esq. United States Attorney for the Northern District of New York. Very Respectfully, yours, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
Instruction Book, p. 3
