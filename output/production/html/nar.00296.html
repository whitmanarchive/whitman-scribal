November 13, 1867. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court United States. Sir: Herewith please find the Record in the case of the United States vs. Julius F. Hartwell, certificate of division of opinion, from the Circuit Court for the District of Massachusetts, for hearing at the ensuing term. Respectfully, &c. John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
