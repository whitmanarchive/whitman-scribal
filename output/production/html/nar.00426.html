February 4, 1868, B. F. Penniman, Esq. Care of Hatch, Estes & Co. p.o. Box 6155, New York City. Sir: Your letter of the 3d, enclosing a copy of your letter of the same date to the Assistant District Attorney at Boston has been received by the Attorney General. I herewith enclose a copy of our Instructions of this date to the United States District Attorney at Boston. Respectfully, your obedient serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant District Attorney. [copy enclosed.]
