May 9, 1868. S. H. Huntington, Esq. Clerk Court of Claims. Sir: I am instructed to acknowledge the receipt by this Office of three additional copies of the 2d Volume of Reports of the Court of Claims. Respectfully, your obedient servant, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
