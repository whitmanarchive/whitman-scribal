March 9, 1871. Hon. William Story, U. S. District Judge, for Western Dist. Arkansas. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a commission appointing you U. S. District Judge for the Western District of Arkansas. You will be please to transmit a copy of your official oath to this Department. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
