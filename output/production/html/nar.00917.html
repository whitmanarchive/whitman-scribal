May 12, 1869. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Sup. Court U. S. Sir: You are requested to send to this office the mandate in the case of the prize stm'r Georgia—also the opinion of the Court in the case of John G. Jackson vs. the Northern Central Railroad Co. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
