January 19, 1869 Messrs. Barr & Goodloe, Attorneys, Louisville, Ky. Gentlemen: The Attorney General directs me to say in reply to your letter of the 12th inst. that its subject matter is under the control of the Commissioner of Internal Revenue, who has not yet asked his advice upon the case. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
