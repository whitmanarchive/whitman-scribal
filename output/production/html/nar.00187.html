Jan. 15, 1867. D. B. Eaton, Esq. Counsellor at Law, New York. Dear Sir: I enclose you a copy of a motion presented to the court in the case of the Steamer Pearl, & cargo,—and will call the same up for August on Friday next at 11 o'clock A. M. Respectfully &c yours, J. Hubley Ashton, Ass't. Att'y. Gen'l.
