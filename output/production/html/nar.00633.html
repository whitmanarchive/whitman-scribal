August 20, 1868. Hon. Hugh McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States, in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. disbursing clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of $3279:12, and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very respectfully, your obd't serv't, Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General
Salaries of the Attorney General, Assistants, Clerks, & Messenger, for August, 1868 ‑ ‑ ‑ 2,529:12
Legal assistance, and other necessary expenditures in the disposal of private land claims in California 500:
Purchase of Law & other necessary Books 250:
$3279:12
