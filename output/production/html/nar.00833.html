March 11, 1869. Hon. Elihu B. Washburne, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to acknowledge the receipt of my commission as Attorney General of the United States, and to inform you that I am a native of Massachusetts. I herewith enclose my oath of allegiance, duly executed. I have the honor to be, your obedient serv't. Ebenezer Rockwood Hoar. Attorney General.
