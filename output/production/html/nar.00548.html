May 28, 1868. Clarence A. Seward, Esq. Counsellor at Law, Box 217, New York City. Sir: Referring to your letters of the 15th and of the 25th instants, and preceding correspondence, respecting the confiscation suit against the interest of Charles M. Morris, I herewith enclose, for your information, a copy of our instructions of this date, to the United States Attorney at New York. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
