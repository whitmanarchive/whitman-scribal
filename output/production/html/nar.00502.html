April 3, 1868. Hon. C. C. Hewitt, Chief Justice, Supreme Court, Washington Ter. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 1st instant, and for the reasons therein stated, I am directed by the Attorney General ad interim to say that you are hereby granted leave of absence until June 1st 1868, as requested. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants Chief Clerk.
