Dec. 4, 1871. Lewis E. Johnson, Esq. U.S. Marshal, Columbia, S.C. Sir: Your letter of the 30th ult. enclosing a list of persons arrested in your District under the authority of the United States, except such as have been committed under precepts of judges or Commissioners, has been received. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
