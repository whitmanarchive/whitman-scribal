January 6, 1868 General U. S. Grant, Secretary of War, ad interim. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith for your information, and such action as you may deem proper, a copy of a letter received this day from the United States Attorney for the Southern District of New York, in reply to a letter from this Office, dated 10th January, 1867, a copy of which is also herewith enclosed. Very respectfully, Your obedient servant, Henry Stanbery Attorney General.
