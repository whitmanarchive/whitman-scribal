Oct. 13, 1871. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State, Sir: I am directed by the President to request you to issue a commission appointing Charles Chesley to be Solicitor of Internal Revenue in place of William Mc Michael, appointed Assistant Attorney General of the United States. This commission to be dated the 15th instant. Very respectfully, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
