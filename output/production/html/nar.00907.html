May 7, 1869. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. No. 535 Chestnut st. Philadelphia Gentlemen: Please find enclosed a check for $576:92, the amount of your corrected bill received this morning. We sent the books to be returned yesterday, by Adams' Express. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
