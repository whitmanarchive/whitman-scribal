January 15, 1867. Hon. Geo. S. Hillard, U. S. Attorney Boston, Mass. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to say that your explanation as to the appointment of Mr. Hyde as your assistant is satisfactory to him, and the account has been approved, & referred to the proper officer of the Treasury Department for settlement. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
