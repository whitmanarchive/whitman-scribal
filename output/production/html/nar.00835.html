March 12, 1869. The Hon. the Secretary of State. Sir: I have examined the papers in reference to the appointment of F. A. Dockray as U. S. Attorney for the Southern Dist. of Florida, & think the Commission should issue. Very resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
