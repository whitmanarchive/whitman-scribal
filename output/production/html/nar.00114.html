Sept. 27, 1866. To E. C. Carrington, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Washington. Sir: I enclose a petition addressed by French Forrest to the President, relative to a proceeding pending in this District against certain property claimed by him. You will please make a full report of the present status of the case, giving all the proceedings that have been had in it. Please return the enclosed letter. Very respectfully, &c &c J. Hubley Ashton, Assistant Atty Gen.
