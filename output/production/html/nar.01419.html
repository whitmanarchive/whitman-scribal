July 19, 1870. Hon. E. R. Hoar, Concord, Mass. Sir: I transmit herewith a draft on the U. S. Assistant Treasurer at New York for $145 51/100, the amount of your salary for seven days in the current month. You will please acknowledge its receipt. Very Respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clk. (draft enc.)
Sending Hon. E. R. Hoar $145:51/100
