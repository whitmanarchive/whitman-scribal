Oct. 27, 1871. Samuel C. Brown, Private Secretary, &c. Trenton, N. J. Sir: I am directed by the Solicitor General and Acting Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 20th instant,—and, in compliance with your request to enclose herewith twenty five (25.) additional blank forms for statistics of crime—and to thank you for your courtesy in this matter. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
