December 20, 1870. John H. Flogg, Esq. Office Secretary U. S. Senate. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 19th instant, I have to inform you that the opinion of the Attorney Gen'l as to the liability of the Pacific Railroad to pay interest on bonds, &c. has not been printed, and therefore it is not in my power to furnish the copies thereof asked for. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l and Acting Attorney General.
