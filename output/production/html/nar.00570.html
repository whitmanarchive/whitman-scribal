June 20, 1868. Hon. H. McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury, Sir: I have the honor to request you to issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Fifteen Hundred and Twenty-Eight Dollars, and Eighty-five cents, ($15,28:85,)— and charge the same to the appropriations indicated below. Very Respectfully, Sir, your obedient servant, OH Browning​ O. H. Browning​ , Attorney General adinterim
