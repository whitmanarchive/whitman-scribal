May 4, 1868. Hon. J. S. Black, Present, Sir: In compliance with your request of the 29th ultimo, I have the honor to furnish you herewith a copy of your Opinion of 19th May, 1857, in relation to a Land Title, in which the Merchants' Exchange Company of Baltimore was concerned, given by you as Attorney General of the United States. Respectfully, your obedient serv't, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General. [opin. enc.]
