Oct. 25, 1871. Hon. James W. Fisher, Cheyenne, Wyoming. Sir: I have the honor to enclose herewith a Commission, appointing you Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of the Territory of Wyoming, and if the same is accepted by you, I will thank you to transmit to this Department a copy of your official oath—and also to inform me of the place of your birth. Very respectfully, B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l. & Acting Attorney Gen'l.
