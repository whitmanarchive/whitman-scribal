Dec. 20, 1871. Hon. H. H. Wells. U. S. Attorney, Richmond, Va. Sir: I have received your letter of the 18th instant, in reference to the case of Patrick McDonald, and concur in your judgment that the case should not be nol. pros'd. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Att'y Gen'l.
Pat. McDonald (? McDonough)
