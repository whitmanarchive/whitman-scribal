October 25, 1867. Alfred Russell, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Detroit, Mich. Sir: A few days since I gave to the Secretary of the Interior my opinion in favor of the payment of your fee of $3,500 as special counsel in S. C. U. S. in re Repentigny. I have just received a letter from the Secretary that he has approved its payment, and has referred it to the 1st Auditor of the Treasury "for adjustment - payment to be made from the Judiciary fund." Very respectfully, Henry Stanberry.
