May 13, 1871. Horatio Jenkins, Esq. Collector Internal Revenue. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 11th instant and to inform you that Claiborn R. Mobley, Esq. of Key West is the Attorney of the United States for the Southern District of Florida. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
Florida letter acknowledged &c
