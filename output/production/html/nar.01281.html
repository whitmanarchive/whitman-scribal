March 29, 1870. Francis C. Barlow, Esq. 5 Beekman street, New York City. Sir: In reply to your letter of the 26th instant, I am instructed to say that the subject-matter has not been referred to this office, and the Attorney General has no jurisdiction in the case. Very resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
