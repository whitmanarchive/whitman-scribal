January 20, 1869. Hon. H. McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request that you will issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Twenty nine hundred and forty eight dollars and one cent ($2948:01,) and charge the same to the appropriation for salaries of the Attorney General, assistant Attorneys Gen'l, Clerks and Messenger of this Office. Respectfully, &c Wm M. Evarts, Attorney General
