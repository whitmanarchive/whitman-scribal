January 10, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to two hundred and fifty dollars, ($250:) of Henry B. Brown, for services as Assistant to Alfred Russell, Esq. United States Attorney for the Eastern District of Michigan, for the quarter ending December 31st, 1867; and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Very Respectfully, your obdt serv't, Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
