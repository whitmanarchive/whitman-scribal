July 6, 1867. Hon. John Wilson, Third Auditor, U. S. Treasury. Sir: At the instance of the Hon. James Speed, I have to request that the papers in the case of Wathan may be transmitted to this office—to be shortly returned. Very Respectfully, &c. &c. John M. Binckley, Ass't. Att'y. Gen'l.
