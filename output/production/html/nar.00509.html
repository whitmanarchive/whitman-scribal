April 10, 1868. R. W. Tayler, Esq. First Comptroller of the Treasury. Sir: I enclose herewith a copy of a letter received from the United States Attorney for the Northern District of Ohio—before acting upon which, it is thought advisable to inquire of you the state of your rulings upon the subject. I would thank you for an early reply. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
