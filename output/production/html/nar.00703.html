November 27, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk, U. S. Supreme Court. Sir: Enclosed please find praecipes for appearance in cases of Flanders, Pl'ff in Error, v. Tweed. The Attorney General requests that you will send copies of the records to this office. Yours respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
