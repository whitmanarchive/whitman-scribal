March 3, 1870. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: In accordance with the request contained in your letter of the 2d inst., I have the honor to return herewith all the papers referred to me, pertaining to the claim of Maria Josefa Cavazas, for rent of the site of Fort Brown. Very respectfully, your obedient servant, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
returning papers in Cavazas' case.
