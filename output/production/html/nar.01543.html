December 14, 1870. Hon. Wm. T. Jones, Asso. Justice Wyoming Territory. Washington, D. C. Sir: After conference with the President, upon your application for leave of absence, I feel authorized to extend your present leave until the 1st of February. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
