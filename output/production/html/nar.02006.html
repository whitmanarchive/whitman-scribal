June 5, 1871. S. A. Brady, Esq. 10 Minetta Lane, New York City. Sir: I am directed by the Solicitor General, and acting Attorney General, to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 13th ultimo, and to enclose to your address a copy of the Report of the Attorney General to Congress in January last—and to inform you that this Department has no copies of printed documents of Congress for distribution. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
