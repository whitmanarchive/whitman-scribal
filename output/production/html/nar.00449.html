March 2, 1868. Hon. William H. Seward, Secretary of State. Sir: In compliance with your request of the 29th ultimo, just received by the Attorney General, I have the honor to enclose herewith all the papers in relation to the vacant Marshalship for the Northern District of New York. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney General.
