April 7, 1869. Hon. W. F. Turner, Chief Justice—Arizona Terr. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney Gen. to say that your request for an extension of your leave of absence is, under the circumstances stated in your letter of the 5th inst. hereby granted, until June 1st, 1869. Very Resp. yours, M. F. Pleasants, Ch Clerk.
