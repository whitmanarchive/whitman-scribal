November 5, 1867. John D. Defrees, Esq. Superintendent Public Printing, Washington, D. C. Sir: You will please have printed and bound in leather cover for the use of this Office, two hundred, (200,) requisitions for Printing, as per copy enclosed. Very respectfully, &c. John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
