November 23, 1867. W. F.M. Arny, Esq. United States Indian Agent, Abiquiu Indian Agency, Rio Arriba Co. New Mexico. Sir: Referring to your communication of the 10th August last, I enclose herewith a copy of the Attorney General's letter of yesterday to the United States Attorney for the District of New Mexico, relative to the Pueblo and the Camanche Indians of that Territory. Respectfully your obd't serv't, John M. Binckley Ass't Attorney Gen'l. [Copy Enc.]
