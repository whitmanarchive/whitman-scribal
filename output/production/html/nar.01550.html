December 16, 1870. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I am directed by the President to request you to issue a Commission appointing Levi Hubbell to the office of Attorney of the United States for the Eastern District of Wisconsin, agreeably to the enclosed resolution of the Senate. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
Commission L. Hubbell.
