March 15, 1869. Dunbar, Waters, & Co, Publishers,[no handwritten text supplied here]Boston, Mass. Gentlemen: The Attorney General desires you to send to this office a copy of the Boston Daily Advertiser. As our bills are paid by the Treasury Dept. if you will send me duplicate receipted bills, I will return the money at once. Respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
