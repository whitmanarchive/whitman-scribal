October 23, 1866. To J. F. & J. M. Linn, Esq's. Attorneys at Law, Lewisburg, Union Co. Penn. Gentlemen: Your letter of 19th inst. is received—and in reply, I have to say that this office cannot make it convenient to take the case to which you refer out of its order upon the calendar. Very respectfully, &c J. Hubley Ashton, Assistant Att'y. Gen'l.
