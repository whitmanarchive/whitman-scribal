July 17, 1868. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court, U. S. Sir: I herewith transmit the record of division of opinion in the case of the United States, vs. Henry C. Dewitt from the Circuit Court of the United States for the Eastern District of Michigan, to be docketed in accordance with the rules of the Court. Respectfully, &c. yours, OH Browning Attorney General ad interim
