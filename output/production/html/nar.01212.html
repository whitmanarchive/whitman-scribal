February 18, 1870. Hon. T. W. Tipton, U. S. Senate. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 17th inst., and to say that no charges have been filed in this office against Judge Reavis, of Arizona Territory. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
