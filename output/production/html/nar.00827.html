March 6, 1869. Hon. J. M. Schofield, Secretary of War. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith a copy of the letter addressed to the District Attorney of South Carolina pursuant to the request contained in your letter of the 2d instant. Very respectfully yours, J. Hubley Ashton, Acting Attorney General. [copy enc.]
