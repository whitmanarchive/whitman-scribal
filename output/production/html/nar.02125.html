Nov. 6, 1871. Nathan Webb, Esq. U. S. Attorney, Portland, Maine. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 3d inst. enclosing therein certain papers relative to the case of Davis R. Stockwell, vs. The United States; and to thank you for the same. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
