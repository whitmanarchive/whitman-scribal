Aug. 7, 1871. Hon. C. Delano, Secretary of the Interior Sir: I have the honor to return herewith the communication of the Commissioner of the General Land Office and other papers which accompanied your letter of the 27th of July last, relating to the suspended land entry of Henry Klnisurgep.94 in the Sioux City, Iowa, land district—and to state that I concur in the views of the Commissioner. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
