November 13, 1869. Hon. W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: In accordance with your request, of the date of yesterday, I have the honor to return herewith the Report of Bv't. Maj. Gen. Alfred H. Terry, U.S.A. dated August 14, 1869. Very Resp. your obdt servt, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
Rep. returned.
