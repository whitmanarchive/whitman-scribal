January 21, 1868. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chairman Judiciary Committee, Senate United States Sir: The District Attorney for the Eastern District of Texas having addressed the Attorney General in a letter in which he states what he supposes to be good reasons against abolishing the said judicial District, it is deemed proper to furnish you with a copy of them, which please find herewith. Respectfully, your obedient servant, John M. Binckley, Assistant Attorney Gen'l.
