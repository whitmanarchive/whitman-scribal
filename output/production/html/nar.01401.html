July 1, 1870. Hon. W. C. Sherrod, House of Representatives. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to say in reply to your letter of the 30th ultimo, that this office has no information on the subject of your inquiry—and to refer you respectfully to the Commissioner of the Census, Dept. of the Interior. Very resp'y yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
