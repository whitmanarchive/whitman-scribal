March 31, 1870. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk United States Sup. Court. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to thank you for your note of the 30th inst., and to say that he does not desire a manuscript copy of the record in the case of Howlett vs. Little and al. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Clerk.
