November 30, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the account, approved by me, and amounting to One thousand dollars, ($1000:) of Hon. Caleb Cushing, for retainer, opinion, and counsel-fee in the case of the United States v. The Meteor—U. S. Supreme Court, December term, 1868; and to request its payment out of the appropriate fund. Yours very respectfully, &c. Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General.
