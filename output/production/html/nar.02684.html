Dec. 26, 1871. George Andrews, Esq. Knoxville, Tenn. Sir: I enclose herewith a commission appointing you to the office of Attorney of the United States for the Eastern District of Tennessee. If the same be accepted, I will thank you to transmit to this Department a copy of your official oath, and also inform me of the place of your nativity. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
