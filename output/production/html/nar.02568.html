Nov. 9, 1871. W. H. Morgan, Esq. Washington, D. C. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 8th inst. and to inform you that he receives visitors daily between the hours of 10 and 11. A. M., and that he will see you on any day you may call between those hours. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
