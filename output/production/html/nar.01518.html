December 6, 1870. Hon W. W. Belknap, Secretary of War. Sir: The District Attorney of Louisiana has been instructed to defend the officers of the United States, in the proceedings which you apprehend from Mr. Wolfley, referred to in your letter to me, of the first instant. Very respectfully, &c, A. T. Akerman, Attorney General.
