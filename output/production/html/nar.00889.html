April 30, 1869. Col. C. M. Alexander, Postmaster, Washington, D. C. Sir: I desire to notify you that Walbridge A. Field has been appointed Assistant Attorney General in the place of Mr. J. Hubley Ashton, resigned, in order that you may give such directions as will enable him to avail himself of the franking privilege according to law. Very Resp. yours, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General
