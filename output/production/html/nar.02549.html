Nov. 2, 1871. J. H. Caldwell, Esq. La Grange, Geo. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General I enclose herewith a draft on New York for $350, being for the amount of your account rendered from September 9 to October 16, 1871. I will thank you to acknowledge its receipt. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
$350 sent.
