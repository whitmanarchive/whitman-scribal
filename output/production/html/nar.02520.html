Oct. 18, 1871. A. D. Robinson, Esq. at Court of Claims Dear Sir: A few days since I sent to Mr. Johnston a letter addressed to this Department by A. Stowe, Esq. of New York, asking the return of certain papers which he had filed with the Attorney General. Will you be kind enough to return Mr. Stowe's letter by to-morrow's mail? Very truly yours, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
