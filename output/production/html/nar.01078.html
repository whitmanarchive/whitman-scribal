November 15, 1869. Hon. Hamilton Fish, Secretary of State. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith, (with request for its return after perusal,) a letter from the United States District Attorney at Philadelphia, enclosing a Report of the U. S. Marshal there, relating to the steamer "General Dulce." Very respectfully, Your obd't serv't, E. R. Hoar, Attorney General.
