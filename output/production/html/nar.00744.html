January 16, 1869. Brewster & Baldwin, No. 786 Broadway, New York City. Gentlemen: Please find enclosed a check on the Assistant Treasurer of the United States in New York for $850: the amount of your bill for carriage & harness—the receipt of which you will please acknowledge. Very respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants Chief Clerk.
