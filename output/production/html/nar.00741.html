January 11, 1869. T. & J. W. Johnson & Co. Philadelphia Gentlemen: The enclosed memorandum is correct. Please send us duplicate receipts, whenever you desire the settlement of an account—when a check will be forwarded at once. Very truly yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
