Dec. 23, 1871. Hon. M. H. Carpenter, U.S. Senate. Sir: Your letter of the 20th is received. I shall send a copy of it to Mr. Davis, and call for an explanation from him. Very respectfully, A. T. Akerman, Attorney Gen'l.
letter (Duluth canal case)
