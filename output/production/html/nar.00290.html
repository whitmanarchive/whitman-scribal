November 12, 1867. D. W. Middleton, Esq. Clerk Supreme Court United States. Sir: Please find herewith the transcript of the Record in the case of Denison, use of U. S., vs Puig Bro's, appealed by the United States from the Circuit Court for the District of Louisiana, for hearing at the ensuing term. Very respectfully, &c. John M.
