June 11, 1868. Hon. John Coburn. House of Representatives. Sir: The Attorney General adinterim directs me to say that your letter of the 10th instant, relative to a claim of Peter Wilkins, said to have been filed in the Adjutant General's Office in 1863, has been received, and referred to the Adjutant General, this office having no information of such claim. Very Respectfully, your obd't serv't, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
