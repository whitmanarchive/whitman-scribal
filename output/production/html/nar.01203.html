February 12, 1870. Hon. W. F. Turner, Chief Justice, &c. Prescott, Arizona Terr. Sir: By direction of the Attorney General, I transmit herewith a copy of a paper filed in this office containing charges against your official conduct, for such answer or notice as you may think proper to give it. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
trans. paper cont. charges.
