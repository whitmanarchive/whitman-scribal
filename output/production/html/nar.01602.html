January 4, 1871. Hon. C. Delano, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I hereby return, with my approval of the adjudication of the Commissioner of the General Land Office, the papers in the matter of the suspended entry of certain lands at the Springfield office, Missouri, by C. W. Barnwell, transmitted to the Attorney General by your letter of the 16th ult. (Z. B.S.) I have the honor to be, &c. B. H. Bristow, Solicitor Gen'l, & act'g Att'y. Gen'l.
