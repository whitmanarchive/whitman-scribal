June 30, 1869. Hon. Geo. S. Boutwell, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: In the Internal Revenue case of Emanuel Marchand, of Ohio, submitted in your letter of the 29th inst., I recommend the acceptance of the compromise proposed by the commissioner of Internal Revenue, namely, that Marchand pay $200.76 as tax, $100 as a specific penalty, and the taxable costs. The papers are returned herewith. I have the honor to be, &c, &c, W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General.
