April 27, 1869. Hon. T. J. Boynton, U. S. Judge, Key West, Fla. The Dist. Attorney was instructed, March 3d, to dismiss libel on "Wren" and take it off the files. Please inform us what has been done in the case. J. Hubley Ashton, Ass't Att'y General. (charge this office.)
