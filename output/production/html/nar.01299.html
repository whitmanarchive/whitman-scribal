April 4, 1870. John C. Grisson, Esq. Dumas Ferry, P. O. Anson Co. N. C. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 24th ultimo, and to say in reply that the U. S. Attorney for North Carolina, D. H. Starbuck, Esq., Salem, N. C. is the proper person for you to apply to for the instructions you desire. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk
Letter acknowledged, &c.
