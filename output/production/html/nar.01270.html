March 24, 1870. R. S. Tharin, Esq. Attorney at Law, Charleston, S. C. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 21st instant in relation to an attempt to embezzle a letter addressed to your client Wm. G. Pinkney; and to say that its subject matter shall have his early attention. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
