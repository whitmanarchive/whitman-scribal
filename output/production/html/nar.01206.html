February 16, 1870. S. H. Hammond, Esq. Dep. Att'y Gen'l. Albany, N. Y. Sir: The Attorney General directs me to acknowledge the receipt of your letter of the 14th inst., requesting to be furnished with rules and regulations relating to Requisitions under the extradition Treaty with France—and to say that he has referred it to the State Department, in accordance with your request. Very Respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Ch. Cl'k.
letter acknowledged.
