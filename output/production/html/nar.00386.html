January 7th, 1868. Hon. O. H. Browning, Secretary of the Interior. Sir: I have the honor to transmit herewith the accounts, approved by me, and amounting to Seven hundred and fifty dollars, ($750:00,) of L. H. Bond, for services as assistant to Durbin Ward, Esq. United States Attorney for the southern District of Ohio, for the quarter ending September 30th, and December 31st, 1867; and to request their payment out of the appropriate fund. Very respectfully, &c. Henry Stanbery, Attorney General.
