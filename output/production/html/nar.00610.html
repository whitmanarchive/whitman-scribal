July 20, 1868. Hon. H. McCulloch, Secretary of the Treasury. Sir: I have the honor to request you to issue a warrant on the Treasurer of the United States, in favor of Bushrod Birch, Esq. Disbursing Clerk, Treasury Department, for the sum of Two thousand dollars, ($2000:00,) and charge the same to the appropriation for the salaries of the Attorney General, Assistant Attorneys General, clerks, and Messenger, for the month of July, 1868. Very respectfully yours, OH Browning, Attorney General ad interim.
