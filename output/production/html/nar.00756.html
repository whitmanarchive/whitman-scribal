January 26, 1869. Hon. D. J. Mould, House of Representatives, Washington. Sir: Your letter of the 25th inst. requesting a copy of an opinion of the Attorney General relative to laws affecting paymasters, has been received. After referring to the office record of opinions, I have to reply that there is no opinion rendered on the subject you mention. Very respectfully, &c. M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
