June 22, 1870 Hon. J. W. Douglass, Act'g Comm'r. of Internal Revenue. Sir: In accordance with your request of this date I have the honor to return herewith the Report of Supervisor Drummond in relation to U. S. Attorney Strickland of Nebraska, dated April 11, 1870. Very respectfully W. A. Field, Acting Attorney General
Report returned
