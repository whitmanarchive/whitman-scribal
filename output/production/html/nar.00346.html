December 11, 1867. Hon. James Lyons, Richmond, Va. Dear Sir: Mr. Chandler has neglected, as yet, to make his report in the Haxall libel case. I will again call attention to the matter, and see that prompt action is had. Very Respectfully yours, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
