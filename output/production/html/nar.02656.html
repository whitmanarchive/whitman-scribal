Dec. 8, 1871. Messrs. Lewis & Fullerton, Attorney's​ Attorneys​ at Law, Washington, D. C. Sirs: In compliance with your request of the 7th instant, I return herewith the letter of Mr. Vaughn, which was transmitted to this Department by you on the 4th instant. Very respectfully, A. J. Falls, Chief Clerk.
