Attorney General's Office, September 1, 1865. Dr. D. W. Yandell, Louisville, Kentucky. Sir: Yours of the 29th ultimo is received; and in reply thereto I am directed by the Attorney General to say that he can see no objection or impropriety in you putting your professional card in the public prints. Very respectfully, W. Stewart, Chief Clerk.
