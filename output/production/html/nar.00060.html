Attorney General's Office, December 15, 1865. To Mr. John Brown, Louisville, Ky. Sir: I am directed by the Attorney General to acknowledge the receipt of yours of the 12th inst. relative to the discharge of Hospital Steward Francis M. Cleaver from the U. S. Army, - and to say that the same has been referred to the Secretary of War, who alone can act in the premises. Very respectfully, Wm. Stewart, Chief Clerk.
