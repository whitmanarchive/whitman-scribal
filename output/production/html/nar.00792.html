Feb. 16, 1869. Hon. B. F. Wade, Pres. pro. tem. U. S. Senate. Sir: In further compliance with the Resolution of the Senate, adopted on the 5th inst. I have the honor to transmit herewith copies of letters relative to the case of the U. S. vs. Vincente P. Gomez, omitted in my communication of the 13th inst. I have the honor to be, &c. &c. &c. Wm. M. Evarts, Attorney General [papers enc.]
letters in case of U. S. vs. V. P. Gomez
