March 17, 1868. Hon. Lyman Trumbull, Chairman Judiciary Com. Senate. Sir: Your note of this date has just been received. By direction of the Attorney General copies of the papers relating to Mr. Rowland's case are now being prepared, and will be forwarded to you forthwith—probably this afternoon. Respectfully &c. John M. Binckly​ Binckley​ Assistant Attorney Gen'l
