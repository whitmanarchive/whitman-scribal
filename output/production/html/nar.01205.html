February 14, 1870. Hon. W. H. Armstrong, House of Representatives. Sir: In reply to your inquiry of the 12th instant, the Attorney General directs me to say that there is no vacancy in the office of Chief Justice of the Territory of New Mexico. Very respectfully, M. F. Pleasants, Chief Clerk.
no vacancy, &c.
